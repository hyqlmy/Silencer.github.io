单词：survey     [ˈsɜrˌveɪ]

翻译：v.&n. 俯瞰，眺望；全面审视，调查；测量图，勘定     释义：a detailed critical inspection
近义词：
  • examine
  • inspect
  • measure
反义词：
    形近词：
    • sure
    例句:
    1. A recent survey of public opinions shows that most people are worried about the increasing crime.

      一份最近的民意调查表明,大多数人对不断增长的犯罪率表示忧虑。

    2. The surveyor made a survey of the land.

      测量员对土地进行了测量。

    3. After much delay, the results of the survey were published.

      耽搁多时之后,调查结果终于公布了。

    4. From the top of the hill you can have a survey of the whole city.

      从山顶上你可以俯瞰整个城市。

    5. Ali was his principal attendant during this nocturnal survey.

      阿里陪着他作这次夜间视察。

    6. They surveyed the damage inflicted by the storm.

      他们调查了暴风雨造成的损失。

    7. She surveyed me haughtily over the top of her glasses.

      她傲慢地从眼镜的上方审视我。

    8. For this book, the author has surveyed recent developments in linguistics.

      为了写这本书,作者研究了语言学目前的发展情况。

    9. They have started to survey the country that the new motorway will pass through .

      他们已开始测量这条新公路将要通过的农村。

    10. The work of surveying and determining the frontier of all the cities and districts went on smoothly.

      我省重新勘定各市、区边界的工作进展顺利。

    11. They sadly surveyed the scene before them.

      他们悲伤地环视眼前的情景。
