单词：result     [riˈzʌlt]

翻译：v. 结果，致使，导致，由…而造成     释义：a phenomenon that follows and is caused by some previous phenomenon;
近义词：
  • arise
  • follow
  • happen
  • issue
  • consequence
  • effect
  • outcome
反义词：
  • cause
形近词：
  • rest
  • resultant
例句:
  1. These problems are the result of years of bad management.

    这些问题是由于多年管理不善而造成的。

  2. The presumable result is an election defeat.

    推测结果是竞选可能会失败。

  3. She looked with pride at the result of her work.

    她自豪地看着自己的工作成果.

  4. If you cube 10, the result is 1000.

    十自乘两次,结果为一千。

  5. He is indifferent to the result of the exam.

    他对考试结果漠不关心。

  6. We desperately need a result from this match.

    这场比赛我们务必获胜。

  7. If this football team doesn't get a result tonight, they will be put down into a lower division.

    如果今晚这支足球队不能赢球，他们将会被降级。

  8. If law and order break down, anarchy will result.

    法治一垮，就会出现无政府状态。

  9. These toxic blooms result from changes in the ocean water.

    这些有毒的菌绒由于海水的变化而产生。

  10. The accident resulted in the death of two passengers.

    这起事故导致两名乘客丧生。
