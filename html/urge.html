单词：urge     [ə:dʒ]

翻译：n. 强烈欲望，迫切要求     释义：an instinctive motive;
近义词：
  • advise
  • force
  • persuade
  • push
反义词：
    形近词：
    • surge
    • urgent
    例句:
    1. He urged the horses on with a whip.

      他用鞭子策马前行。

    2. Many physicians urge aerobic exercise.

      很多医生鼓励有氧运动。

    3. We will urge them to adhere to the Paris Agreement.

      我们将敦促他们遵守巴黎协议。

    4. They urged on us the need for cooperation.

      他们向我们强调合作的必要性。

    5. I strongly urge you to give up smoking.

      我力劝你戒烟。

    6. He urged against the adoption of the measure.

      他极力反对采取这项措施。

    7. I feel the urge to be outside when spring comes again.

      当春天再次来临,我就有了到户外活动的冲动。

    8. An urge to improve society led me into politics.

      推动社会进步的强烈愿望使我投身于政治。

    9. When the urge to create a cellar happens it's usually at the beginning of your wine appreciation career.

      当你有强烈愿望要建一个酒窖时，通常是你的葡萄酒鉴赏生涯的开始。
