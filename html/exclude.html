单词：exclude     [iksˈklu:d]

翻译：v. 拒绝，把…排除在外，排斥     释义：prevent from being included or considered or accepted;
近义词：
  • preclude
  • refuse
反义词：
  • accept
  • include
形近词：
  • preclude
例句:
  1. At this stage we cannot entirely exclude the possibility of staff cuts.

    在目前这个阶段,我们还不能完全排除裁员的可能性。

  2. I mean the last thing we would think about is to exclude the children from this plan.

    我的意思是,我们绝不会考虑把孩子们排除在这个计划之外的。

  3. Thick curtains help to exclude street noises.

    厚窗帘有助于隔绝街上的噪音。

  4. The court excluded the improperly obtained evidence.

    法院不接受非正常渠道获得的证据
