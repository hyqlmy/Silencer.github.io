单词：frighten     [ˈfraitən]

翻译：v. 使惊恐     释义：cause fear in;
近义词：
  • panic
  • scare
  • startle
  • terrify
反义词：
  • calm
  • reassure
形近词：
  • fright
  • freight
例句:
  1. It is illegal to frighten a pigeon.

    惊吓鸽子是违法的。

  2. They tried to frighten the public into obedience.

    他们企图吓唬群众使他们屈服。

  3. He frightened the old man into giving him all the money.

    他恐吓那位老人把所有的钱都给了他。

  4. You frighten me, even though you're gentle with me.

    你使我害怕,尽管你对我很温柔。

  5. The little baby was frightened by the fierce dog.

    那个小孩被恶狗吓坏了。

  6. You'll find that I don't frighten easily.

    你会发现我不是轻易就会害怕的。
