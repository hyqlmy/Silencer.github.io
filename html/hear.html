单词：hear     [hiə]

翻译：v. 听见；审讯；（from）收到…的信/电话；听说     释义：perceive (sound) via the auditory sense
近义词：
  • attend
  • give an ear to
  • heed
  • listen
反义词：
  • be deaf to
  • pay no notice
  • turn a deaf ear to
形近词：
  • shear
  • ear
  • heart
例句:
  1. How did you hear about us? How did you hear about this job?你是如何得知我们的?

    如何得知这个工作的?

  2. Have you heard the news?

    你听到那消息了吗？

  3. I heard (that) he was ill.

    我听说他病了。

  4. We'd better hear what they have to say.

    我们还是听听他们要说些什么吧。

    我听见有人笑。

    你听到他出去了吗?

  7. He was heard to groan.

    有人听见他在呻吟。

  8. The court heard the evidence.

    法庭听取了证词.

    哪位法官（将）审理这案件？
