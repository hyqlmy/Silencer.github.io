单词：drip     [drip]

翻译：n. 滴，水滴，点滴     释义：flowing in drops; the formation and falling of drops of liquid;
近义词：
  • drop
反义词：
    形近词：
    • rip
    • dip
    例句:
    1. A drip of water fell from the tap.

      从龙头滴下一滴水。

    2. The patient is on a drip.

      病人正在输液。

    3. The patient is put on a drip after her operation.

      那个病人动过手术后就打点滴。

    4. Don't be such a drip! Come and join in the fun.

      别呆在那里了! 过来一起玩儿吧。

    5. As water continues to drip, the icicle grows.

      随着水不断下滴，冰柱的体积也慢慢增大。

    6. Water is dripping from the pipe.

      水从管中滴落。

    7. The rainwater drips from the eaves.

      雨水从屋檐滴下。

    8. They drip in fat, and they do nothing.

      他们富得流油，而且什么都不干。
