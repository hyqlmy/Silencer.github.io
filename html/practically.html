单词：practically     [ˈpræktikəli]

翻译：adv. 几乎，实际上，简直     释义：almost; nearly;
近义词：
  • actually
  • really
  • virtually
反义词：
  • theoretically
形近词：
  • practical
例句:
  1. Bread was practically non-existent.

    实际上没有面包。

  2. Practically, the plan didn't work well.

    实际上该计划进行得不顺利。

  3. He is practically ruined.

    他几乎破产了。

  4. The town was practically deserted.

    该镇几乎已空无一人。

  5. The car has practically fallen apart.

    汽车简直已经四分五裂了。

  6. What I said made practically no impression on her.

    我的话简直对她不起作用。
