单词：adverse     [ˈædvə:s]

翻译：adj. 逆的，相反的；敌对的；不利的；有害的     释义：
近义词：
  • contrary
  • hurtful
  • opposite
  • unlucky
反义词：
  • favorable
  • friendly
形近词：
  • diverse
  • verse
例句:
  1. The judge gave us an adverse decision.

    法官作出对我们不利的判决。

  2. He ignored all kinds of adverse comments about him.

    他不理睬各种各样对他不利的评论。

  3. The development was adverse to our interests.

    这种发展与我们的利益背道而驰。

  4. Diseases are adverse to the best growth of children.

    疾病对儿童的健康成长是有害的。
