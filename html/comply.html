单词：comply     [kəmˈplai]

翻译：v. （with）遵照，照做，应允；顺从，服从     释义：act in accordance with someone's rules, commands, or wishes;
近义词：
  • conform
  • follow
  • obey
反义词：
  • disobey
  • oppose
  • reject
形近词：
  • copy
  • compel
例句:
  1. She was told to pay the fine, but refused to comply.

    通知她交纳罚款，但她拒不服从。

  2. He was willing to comply with my proposal.

    他愿意顺从我的建议。

  3. Everyone should comply with the law.

    每个人都应该遵守法律。

  4. Is it our duty to comply with his requests?

    我们有义务满足他的要求吗?

  5. I am sorry I am unable to comply with your request.

    很抱歉，我不能答应你的请求。
