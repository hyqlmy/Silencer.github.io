单词：disappear     [ˌdisəˈpiə]

翻译：v. 不见，消失     释义：get lost, as without warning or explanation;
近义词：
  • fade
  • vanish
反义词：
  • appear
  • emerge
形近词：
  • appear
例句:
  1. The sun disappeared behind a cloud.

    太阳消失在一片云后面。

  2. Many beautiful fish are fast disappearing because of the severe pollution.

    因为污染严重，许多美丽的鱼类正在面临绝种。

  3. My wallet has disappeared from the table.

    我放在桌子上的钱包不见了。

  4. I saw her step the street and disappear into the post-office.

    我看见她在街上步行，进了邮局就不见了。
