单词：rarely     [ˈrɛəli]

翻译：adv. 很少，难得，非常地     释义：not often;
近义词：
  • ever
  • hardly
  • not often
  • seldom
反义词：
  • always
  • continually
  • frequently
  • often
形近词：
  • rare
  • barely
  • rely
例句:
  1. She rarely went anywhere except to her office.

    她除了去办公室以外, 很少去别的地方。

  2. Rarely did he fail to keep his promise.

    他真的很难得失信。

  3. Rarely have I seen such a scene.

    我难得看到这样的场面。

  4. You shouldn't miss the opportunity to see the play-it's rarely put on.

    你不要错过看那出戏的机会,它可是难得上演。
