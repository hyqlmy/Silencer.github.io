单词：effective     [iˈfektiv]

翻译：adj. 有效的，生效的；给人深刻印象     释义：producing or capable of producing an intended result or having a striking effect;
近义词：
  • efficient
  • capable
  • impressive
反义词：
  • ineffective
  • incompetent
  • useless
形近词：
  • effect
例句:
  1. Advertising is often the most effective method of promotion.

    做广告往往是最有效的推销方法。

  2. Their efforts to improve the production have been very effective.

    他们提高生产力的措施卓有成效。

  3. When does the new system become effective?

    新制度何时生效？

  4. Her brother is the king, but she is the effective ruler.

    她的兄弟是国王, 但她才是实际的主宰者。

  5. Last year saw a decline in the effective demand for steel.

    去年,钢铁的实际需求下降了。
