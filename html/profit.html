单词：profit     [ˈprɔfit]

翻译：v. （by，from）得利，获益；有利于     释义：the excess of revenues over outlays in a given period of time (including depreciation and other non-cash expenses)
近义词：
  • advantage
  • benefit
  • gain
  • interest
反义词：
  • break even
  • lose
形近词：
    例句:
    1. There is very little profit in selling newspapers at present.

      现在卖报纸利润很少。

    2. I want to know the net profit.

      我想知道净收益。

    3. It is well known that fake and inferior commodities do harm to the profit of consumers.

      众所周知,假冒伪劣商品损害了消费者的利益。

    4. We profit from the work of the specialist.

      我们从专家的工作中得到益处。

    5. He learned to profit by his mistakes.

      他学会了从自己的错误中获益。

    6. Knowing what you know and profit from it.

      知道自己知道什么,并从中获利。

    7. All his wealth did not profit him.

      他所有的财富于他无益。
