单词：scarcely     [ˈskɛəsli]

翻译：adv. 几乎不，简直没有，勉强     释义：only a very short time before;
近义词：
  • hardly
反义词：
    形近词：
    • scarce
    例句:
    1. He spoke scarcely a word of English.

      他几乎连一个英文单词都不会说。

    2. We scarcely ever used the reserve generator.

      我们几乎从未用过这台备用发动机

    3. This book is scarcely worthy reading.

      这本书简直不值得阅读。

    4. I could scarcely recognize him.

      我简直认不出他来了。

    5. Scarcely had he arrived when he had to leave again.

      他刚刚到达就不得不又离开。

    6. They could scarcely complain after such good treatment.

      在受到如此好的待遇后他们决不会抱怨。
