单词：taxi     [ˈtæksi]

翻译：v. [指飞机]（使）滑行     释义：a car driven by a person whose job is to take passengers where they want to go in exchange for money
近义词：
  • cab
  • taxicab
反义词：
    形近词：
    • tax
    例句:
    1. Let me go shares with you in the taxi fare.

      我和你分摊计程车费吧。

    2. If you want to ensure that you catch the plane, take a taxi.

      你要想确保能赶上那班飞机，就坐出租车去吧。

    3. That may be our taxi now!那辆可能就是我们的计程车了。

    4. I got in a taxi and rushed to the company.

      我搭了一辆计程车赶往公司。

    5. Queue here for a taxi.

      等候出租汽车在此排队。

    6. Please taxi documents to the law office.

      请用出租汽车把文件送到律师事务所。

    7. I would taxi home and sleep till eight.

      我会坐出租车回家，一直睡到八点。

    8. You can taxi back to the apron via taxiway No.5.

      你可以通过五号滑行道回到停机坪。
