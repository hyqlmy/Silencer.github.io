单词：sequence     [ˈsi:kwəns]

翻译：n. 先后，次序；连续，数列     释义：serial arrangement in which things follow in logical order or a recurrent pattern;
近义词：
  • chain
  • cycle
  • series
反义词：
  • severance
形近词：
  • consequence
例句:
  1. We will deal with events in historical sequence.

    我们将按照历史上的先后顺序研究这些事件。

  2. She made a sequence of dance movements.

    她做了一个连续的舞蹈动作。

  3. He described the events of that day in sequence.

    他按先后次序描述了那一天发生的种种事件。

  4. A sequence of murders had prompted the police to actions.

    一连串的谋杀迫使警方不得不采取行动。

  5. Please sequence the names in alphabetical order.

    请按字母顺序排列好名单。
