单词：definite     [ˈdefinit]

翻译：adj. 明确的；一定的；意志坚强的，立场坚定的     释义：precise; explicit and clearly defined;
近义词：
  • certain
  • clear
  • obvious
反义词：
  • indefinite
  • undetermined
形近词：
  • finite
  • deficit
  • define
例句:
  1. His vague ideas crystallized into a definite plan.

    他那些模糊的想法变成了一个明确的计划。

  2. The workers demand a definite answer.

    工人们要求一个明确的答复。

  3. You have our definite support.

    我们绝对支持您。

  4. It's definite that he will win.

    他肯定会赢。

  5. Is it definite that they will sign that contract?

    他们肯定会签署这份合同吗？
