单词：forthcoming     [fɔ:θˈkʌmiŋ]

翻译：n. 来临     释义：at ease in talking to others
近义词：
  • impending
  • prospective
反义词：
    形近词：
    • shortcoming
    例句:
    1. He was exhilarateed by the thought of his forthcoming trip.

      他想到即将到来的旅行，就兴高采烈。

    2. Do you have a list of forthcoming books?

      你有即将问世书本的目录吗?

      我们要的钱尚未得到。

    4. She's never very forthcoming about her plans.

      她一直不很愿意说出自己的计划。
