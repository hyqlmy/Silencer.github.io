单词：satisfactory     [ˌsætisˈfæktəri]

翻译：adj. 令人满意的，圆满的，良好的，符合要求的     释义：giving satisfaction;
近义词：
  • acceptable
  • fair
  • suitable
反义词：
  • inadequate
  • unacceptable
  • unsatisfactory
形近词：
  • satisfaction
例句:
  1. The efficacy of the medicine is satisfactory.

    这药的药效令人满意。

  2. His general state of health is fairly satisfactory.

    他总的健康状况相当令人满意。

  3. You must accept the result, satisfactory or otherwise.

    你必须接受这个结果，满意也好不满意也好。

  4. Everything was turning out most satisfactory.

    一切都非常趁心如意。

  5. The little that I have seen of his work is satisfactory.

    他的工作就我所见的那一小部分而言是令人满意的。

  6. The answer was not quite satisfactory to him.

    这项答复未能使他完全满意。

  7. Did he provide a satisfactory explanation?

    他是否给出了令人满意的解释？
