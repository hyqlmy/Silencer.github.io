单词：vigorous     [ˈviɡərəs]

翻译：adj. 朝气蓬勃的，精力旺盛的     释义：characterized by forceful and energetic action or activity;
近义词：
  • active
  • energetic
  • strong
反义词：
    形近词：
    • rigorous
    例句:
    1. Old as he is, he is none the less vigorous and active.

      虽然他年纪很大了，他仍然精力充沛。

    2. He never ceased to chase after his dream in his vigorous youth.

      在精力充沛的年轻时代他从未停止过追求自己的梦想。

    3. She gave the floor a vigorous sponge all over.

      她用海绵把地板 使劲儿擦了一遍。

    4. He uttered several vigorous expletives when he dropped the iron on his foot.

      他的熨斗掉下砸在脚上，他随口骂了几声。

    5. These corn plants are very vigorous.

      这些玉米秧正茁壮生长。
