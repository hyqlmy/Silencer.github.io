单词：accommodate     [əˈkɔmədeit]

翻译：vt. 向…提供住处；容纳，接纳；顾及     释义：be agreeable or acceptable to;
近义词：
  • provide
  • supply
  • adapt
  • adjust
反义词：
    形近词：
    • accommodation
    例句:
    1. This hotel can accommodate up to 500 guests.

      这旅馆可供500位来宾住宿。

    2. We must accommodate ourselves to circumstances.

      我们必须适应环境。

    3. One flat can accommodate a family of five.

      一套单元房可容纳一个五口之家。

    4. The bank is accommodating its customers more than it used to.

      这家银行现在给客户贷款比以前多了。

    5. The government came up with a new economic proposal that accommodates the special needs and interests of the elderly.

      政府出台了一项新的考虑老年人特殊要求和利益的新经济提案。

    6. He tried to accommodate the dispute between his two friends.

      他尽力调解两个朋友之间的纠纷。

    7. Her eyes took a while to accommodate to the darkness.

      过了一会儿，她的眼睛才能适应黑暗。
