单词：violate     [ˈvaiəleit]

翻译：vt. 违背；冒犯；侵犯；亵渎     释义：fail to agree with; be in violation of; as of rules or patterns;
近义词：
  • break
  • infringe
反义词：
  • keep
  • obey
  • observe
形近词：
  • isolate
例句:
  1. Don't violate other people's copyright.

    不要侵犯他人版权。

  2. I'm committed not to violate the rules.

    我有义务不违反规定。

  3. The country violated the international agreement.

    这个国家违反了国际协议。

  4. The sound of guns violated the peace of the countryside.

    炮声扰乱了乡村的宁静。

  5. The soldiers violated the church by using it as a stable.

    士兵们把教堂当马厩用而亵渎了它。
