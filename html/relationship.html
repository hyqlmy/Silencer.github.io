单词：relationship     [riˈleiʃənʃip]

翻译：n. 关系，联系     释义：a relation between people; (`relationship' is often used where `relation' would serve, as in `the relationship between inflation and unemployment', but the preferred usage of `relationship' is for human relations or states of relatedness);
近义词：
  • bond
  • connection
反义词：
    形近词：
    • relation
    例句:
    1. Part of my job is to maintain good relationship with our suppliers.

      我的部分工作是同我们的供应商保持良好的关系。

    2. We have a working relationship.

      我们是工作上的关系。

    3. I'm not going to give my son the job just because of our relationship.

      我不打算把这个职位给我的儿子,正因为我们有这层亲属关系。

    4. Is there any relationship between them?

      它们之间有联系吗?
