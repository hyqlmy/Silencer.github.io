单词：neglect     [niˈɡlekt]

翻译：v.&n. 忽视；疏忽，漏做，忽略     释义：lack of attention and due care
近义词：

  • idleness
  • negligence
  • slight
反义词：
  • attention
  • care
  • notice
  • regard
形近词：
  • elect
例句:
  1. You shouldn't neglect your health.

    你不该忽略你的健康。

  2. No country can afford to neglect education.

    任何国家都不容忽视教育。

  3. He neglected to write and say "Thank you".

    他忘了写信道谢。

  4. She was severely criticized for neglect of duty.

    她因玩忽职守而受到严厉批评。

  5. We cannot leave aside his neglect of duty.

    对他的玩忽职守我们不能听之任之。

  6. The house has fallen into a state of neglect.

    这所房屋已处于荒废的状态。
