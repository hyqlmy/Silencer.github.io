单词：provided     [prəˈvaidid]

翻译：conj. 倘若，只要，假如     释义：give something useful or necessary to
近义词：
  • if
  • supposing
反义词：
    形近词：
    • provide
    例句:
    1. I shall go provided that it doesn't rain.

      假如不下雨我就去。

    2. Provided that you reduce the price, I'll take all the goods.

      假如你减价,我会买下所有的货物。

    3. Provided we have adequate supplies, the enemy can never break in.

      若有充足的给养，敌人是打不进来的。

    4. I will come provided that I am well enough.

      只要我身体好一定来。

    5. Provided that circumstances permit, we shall hold the meeting next week.

      只要情况允许，我们下周将举行会议。

    6. Please put your litter in the bin provided.

      请你把废物扔到预备好的垃圾箱里。

    7. To get these features working you first have to install the software provided.

      获得这些功能的工作,你首先必须安装该软件提供的。

    8. The newspaper article provided him with fuel for his speech.

      报纸上的这篇文章为他的演讲提供了启发。

    9. Many social services are still provided by voluntary societies in our city.

      在我们的城市，许多社会服务仍然是有志愿团体提供的。

    10. The recent scandal has provided the government's opponents with plenty of ammunition.

      最近的丑闻给政府的反对派提供了大量的炮弹。
