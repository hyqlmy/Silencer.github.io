单词：despair     [disˈpɛə]

翻译：v. 绝望     释义：a state in which all hope is lost or absent;
近义词：
  • discouragement
  • hopelessness
反义词：
  • hope
  • joy
形近词：
    例句:
    1. A person is most disappointed when he is in despair.

      一个人在绝望时最失望。

    2. He has been in the abyss of despair.

      他已陷入绝望的深渊。

    3. He fluctuated between hope and despair.

      他时而充满希望时而失望。

    4. He is the despair of all other pianists.

      他使其他所有的钢琴家都感到失望。

    5. We cannot despair of humanity, since we are ourselves human beings.

      身为人类,我们不能对人性失望。

    6. They despaired of winning the game.

      他们不抱比赛得胜的希望。
