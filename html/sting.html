单词：sting     [stiŋ]

翻译：v.&n. 刺，刺痛，剧痛；刺，叮     释义：a kind of pain; something as sudden and painful as being stung;
近义词：
  • pierce
  • prick
  • stab
反义词：
  • caress
形近词：
  • setting
  • swing
  • sing
  • thing
  • stain
  • string
例句:
  1. Her ingratitude stung him.

    她的忘恩负义使他痛心。

  2. The smoke began to sting his eyes.

    烟开始刺痛他的眼睛。

  3. Those harsh words stung me bitterly.

    那些严厉的言辞使我感到非常痛苦

  4. They used to sting their clients.

    他们过去常诈骗客户。

  5. It is the nature of the scorpion to sting .

    蝎子生下来就会蛰人。

  6. His knee stung from the graze.

    他的膝盖擦伤后十分疼痛。

  7. A bee pricks the skin with its sting.

    蜜蜂用螫针刺皮肤。

  8. Put mud on the sting to take away the pain.

    放泥在刺痛处可以减轻疼痛。

  9. His flattery carries a sting.

    他的奉承话中带刺。

  10. The bitterness was from old habit, and had no sting in it.

    这痛苦他已经习惯了，没有讽刺意味了。
