单词：impressive     [imˈpresiv]

翻译：adj. 给人深刻印象的，感人的     释义：making a strong or vivid impression;
近义词：
  • effective
  • exciting
  • touching
反义词：
  • unimpressive
形近词：
  • impression
  • impress
例句:
  1. His collection of paintings is the most impressive.

    他的绘画收藏令人叹为观止。

  2. The report looks impressive but it's really nothing but waffle.

    这篇报告貌似冠冕堂皇，实际上空洞无物。

  3. Among his many good qualities, punctuality is the most impressive one.

    在他众多优秀品质中,守时给人留下了最深刻印象。

  4. Their success is all the more impressive when you consider the efforts they've made.

    如果你考虑到他们所付出的努力,你就会觉得他们的成功真的很了不起。
