单词：link     [liŋk]

翻译：n. 环节，链环     释义：the means of connection between things linked in series
近义词：
  • connect
  • join
反义词：
  • separate
形近词：
  • ink
例句:
  1. Television stations around the world are linked by satellite.

    全世界的电视台通过卫星联系一起。

  2. The road links all the new towns.

    这条路把所有的新城镇都连接起来了。

  3. He detached a link from a chain.

    他从链子上拆下一个链环。

  4. Is there a link between smoking and lung disease?

    吸烟与肺部疾病之间有关系吗？

  5. We must question the value of our link with the university.

    我们要斟酌一下与这所大学联系有何价值。

  6. We must take firm hold the key link of our work.

    我们要牢牢抓住工作的主要环节。
