单词：purpose     [ˈpə:pəs]

翻译：n. 目的，意图；用途，效果     释义：an anticipated outcome that is intended or that guides your planned actions;

    2. He was a man of purpose.

      他是一个意志坚强的人

      我敢断定,他这次来意图不轨。
