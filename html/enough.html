单词：enough     [iˈnʌf]

翻译：adv. 足够地     释义：an adequate quantity; a quantity that is large enough to achieve a purpose;
近义词：
  • abundant
  • adequate
  • plenty
反义词：
  • inadequate
形近词：
    例句:
    1. I haven't enough time for reading.

      我没有足够的时间读书。

    2. He has enough money (=money enough) to buy a car.

      他有足够的钱买一辆汽车。

    3. John's old enough to take care of himself.

      约翰长大了,能照顾自己了。

    4. He is brave enough to face dangers.

      他很勇敢,足以面对各种危险。

    5. He is competent enough to fill that position.

      他完全胜任那个职位。

    6. He was careful enough to check up every detail.

      他非常仔细，把每一个细节都核对过了。

    7. He seemed pleasant enough to me.

      他对我似乎已相当和气了。

    8. I've had enough of that.

      我真受够了。

    9. I'm afraid I've eaten more than enough.

      我怕我是吃得过多了。
