单词：agony     [ˈæɡəni]

翻译：n. （精神或肉体的）极大痛苦，创伤     释义：intense feelings of suffering; acute mental or physical pain;
近义词：
  • torment
反义词：
  • comfort
  • relief
形近词：
  • wagon
例句:
  1. It was great agony to watch him passing away.

    看着他死去是极大的痛苦。

  2. The agony of that moment was terrific.

    那一刹那的痛苦是可怕的。

  3. He was in an agony of remorse.

    他处于悔恨的海洋中。

  4. Rescue the jobber girl who struggles in agony.

    救救痛苦中挣扎的打工妹。

  5. Thousands upon thousands of others had lingered on to die in slow agony.

    成千上万的另一些人在痛苦中挣扎之后慢慢死去。
