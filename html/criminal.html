单词：criminal     [ˈkriminəl]

翻译：adj. 犯罪的，刑事的     释义：someone who has committed a crime or has been legally convicted of a crime
近义词：
  • convict
  • lawbreaker
反义词：
  • civil
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The criminal was sentenced to prison for theft.

      那个犯人因偷窃被判入狱。

    2. Murder and stealing are criminal acts.

      谋杀和偷盗都是犯罪行为。

    3. I have no criminal record in my whole life.

      我一生无犯罪纪录。

    4. They were reinvestigating a criminal case.

      他们正在复查一桩刑事案件。

    5. We are placing your son in criminal detention.

      我们对你儿子实行刑事拘留。

    6. The government has adopted practical measures for handling these kinds of criminal offenses according to law.

      中国采取了切实措施并依法严格查处这类违法犯罪案件。
