单词：prompt     [prɔmpt]

翻译：v. 激起，促进，推动     释义：a cue given to a performer (usually the beginning of the next line to be spoken);
近义词：
  • quick
  • ready
  • timely
反义词：
  • later
  • slow
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The occasion calls for prompt action.

      这场合需要迅速的行动。

    2. Their prompt actions prevented the fire from spreading.

      他们敏捷的行动阻止了火势的蔓延。

    3. Thank you for your prompt reply to our telegram.

      多谢您对我们电报的即刻答复。

    4. His curiosity prompted him to ask questions .

      他的好奇心激起他问许多问题。

    5. These findings prompted worldwide research on factors controlling the inactivation of viruses in marine waters.

      这些发现促进了世界范围对海洋水体中控制病毒不复活因素的研究。

    6. Science and technology has prompted the rapid development of modern society.

      科学技术推动了现代社会的迅速发展。

    7. She needed to be occasionally prompted.

      她需要不时给予提示。

    8. Will you prompt for us at the next performance?

      下场演出时你给我们提词好吗?

    9. The speaker was rather hesitant and had to be given a frequent prompt by the chairman.

      那个发言人讲话结结巴巴的,经常得由主席给个提示。
