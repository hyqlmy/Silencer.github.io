单词：chance     [tʃɑ:ns]

翻译：v. 碰巧，偶然发生     释义：a possibility due to a favorable combination of circumstances;
近义词：
  • accident
  • fate
  • luck
  • opportunity
  • possibility
  • prospect
反义词：
  • certainty
  • inevitability
形近词：
  • cancer
  • change
  • cancel
例句:
  1. That would be a daunting task with little chance of success.

    那样做的话任务过于艰巨,且成功的可能性微乎其微。

  2. Is there any chance of getting tickets for tonight's performance?

    有可能得到今晚演出的票吗?

  3. Please give me a chance to explain.

    请给我个机会让我解释一下。

  4. I never miss a chance of playing football.

    我从不错过踢足球的机会。

  5. We may lose a lot of support, but that's a chance we'll have to take.

    我们或许会失去很多支持，但这个风险我们必须冒一冒。

  6. I met her quite by chance.

    我遇见她完全是偶然的。

  7. A chance meeting brought the two old friends together again.

    偶然的相遇使两位老朋友重新聚首。

  8. If it should chance to rain we'll take a taxi home.

    万一要下雨的话，我们就乘出租车回家。

  9. Perhaps you might chance on the dictionary at some old bookstall.

    或许你会碰巧在旧书摊上买到这本辞典。

  10. We were very lucky to chance on him in the reading room.

    我们非常幸运地在阅览室里遇到了他。

  11. It may be risky but we will chance it anyhow.

    这可能有危险, 但我们无论如何要冒一冒险。

  12. We'll have to chance meeting an enemy patrol.

    我们不得不冒著可能遇上敌人巡逻兵的危险。
