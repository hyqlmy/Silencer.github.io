单词：competitive     [kəmˈpetitiv]

翻译：adj. 竞争的；好竞争的；（价格等的）有竞争力的     释义：involving competition or competitiveness;
近义词：
  • aggressive
  • ambitious
  • keen
反义词：
  • unambitious
形近词：
  • competition
例句:
  1. It's getting very competitive in the car industry.

    汽车工业方面的竞争日益加剧。

  2. Nobody can entirely keep away from this competitive world.

    没有人能够完全远离这个竞争社会。

  3. Ford has a more competitive environment.

    福特公司具有一个较有竞争力的环境。

  4. Our prices are competitive.

    我们的价格是有竞争性的。

  5. Our firm is no longer competitive in world markets.

    我们公司在世界市场上已不占优势。
