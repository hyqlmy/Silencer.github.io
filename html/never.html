单词：never     [ˈnevə]

翻译：adv. 永不，从不，决不；从来没有；不，没有     释义：not ever; at no time in the past or future;
近义词：
  • at no time
  • not at all
  • on no account
反义词：
  • always
  • forever
形近词：
  • nerve
  • lever
  • fever
  • every
  • ever
例句:
  1. I will never forget your kindness.

    我永远忘不了你的好意。

  2. In these altitudes snow never melts.

    在这样的高度，雪永不融化。

  3. His love for science will never die.

    他对科学的热爱永不会消退。

  4. We will never yield to invaders.

    我们绝不会向侵略者屈服。

  5. We never flinch from difficulties.

    在困难面前我们决不退缩。

  6. She would never thrash her children.

    她绝不会鞭打她的孩子。

  7. She never goes to the cinema.

    她向来不看电影。

  8. I never miss a chance of playing football.

    我从不错过踢足球的机会。

  9. He had no sense of shame and never felt guilty.

    他毫无羞耻之心，而且从不觉得内疚。

  10. He has never heard of that before.

    他未曾听说过那事。

  11. I have never made his acquaintance.

    我与他素昧平生。
