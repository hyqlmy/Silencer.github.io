单词：gasp     [ɡɑ:sp]

翻译：v. 喘息；气吁吁地说     释义：a short labored intake of breath with the mouth open;
近义词：
  • catch the breath
  • pant
  • sudden short breath
反义词：
    形近词：
    • grasp
    • gap
    • gas
    例句:
    1. He gasped out the message.

      他喘着气说出了这个消息。

    2. I gasped as I jumped into the cold river.

      当我跳进冰冷的河里时，简直透不过气来。

    3. I gasped with surprise at the unexpected news.

      听到意外的消息，我惊讶地吸了一口气。

    4. The exhausted runner was gasping for air.

      那人跑得疲惫已极，上气不接下气。

    5. I was gasping for a cigarette.

      我很想抽支烟。
