单词：exclusive     [iksˈklu:siv]

翻译：adj. 独占的；孤高的；唯一的；高级的，奢华的     释义：a news report that is reported first by one news organization;
近义词：
  • sole
  • undivided
反义词：
  • general
  • inclusive
形近词：
  • excuse
例句:
  1. He is part of an exclusive social circle and belongs to an exclusive club.

    他所处的社交圈子很排外，而且参加的又是个很排外的社团。

  2. The interview is exclusive to this magazine.

    本杂志得到独家访问权。

  3. Painting has not been her exclusive occupation.

    绘画并非她唯一的职业.

  4. The teacher tried to gain the students' exclusive attention.

    老师努力试着获得学生的全部注意力。

  5. She often visits the exclusive shops and buys a lot of luxuries.

    她经常光顾高档品商店,买许多奢侈品。

  6. Everyone was surprised by the Times exclusive about the prince' marriage.

    泰晤士报关于王子大婚的独家新闻令很多人惊奇不已。

  7. Our group will have exclusive use of a 60-foot boat.

    我们这组将拥有一艘60英尺长的船的单独使用权。
