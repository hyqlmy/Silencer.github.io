单词：badly     [ˈbædli]

翻译：adv. 非常，严重地；坏地，差地，拙劣地     释义：to a severe or serious degree;
近义词：
  • carelessly
  • unfairly
反义词：
  • mildly
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The firm did badly last year.

      公司去年生意不好。

    2. The child behaved badly at the party.

      那孩子在聚会上的表现很差

    3. He was wounded badly.

      他伤得很厉害。

    4. She was injured badly in the accident.

      她在这次意外中受到重伤。

    5. Traffic was badly snarled up near the accident.

      车祸现场附近交通极为混乱。

    6. I never dreamt that he would behave so badly.

      我做梦都没想到他会表现得如此恶劣。

    7. She wants the doll badly.

      她非常想要这个洋娃娃。

    8. The bad news has shaken her up pretty badly.

      那坏消息一直使她感到极度不安。
