单词：dominant     [ˈdɔminənt]

翻译：adj. 支配的，统治的，占优势的     释义：(music) the fifth note of the diatonic scale
近义词：
  • chief
  • influential
  • main
  • powerful
反义词：
  • recessive
  • subordinate
形近词：
  • predominant
  • dominate
例句:
  1. Peace was the dominant theme of the conference.

    和平是大会的首要议题。

  2. He is a dominant leader in our university.

    他是我们学校最主要的一位领导。

  3. The right hand is dominant in most people.

    大多数人的右手是优势手。

  4. The company can't preserve its dominant position in the market.

    该公司在市场上不能让自己保持优势地位。

  5. The disease is under the control of a single dominant gene.

    这种疾病受一种单一显性基因的控制。

  6. We should be the dominant of ourselves.

    我们要做自己的主宰者。

  7. Genes are described as DOMINANT or RECESSIVE.

    基因分为显性基因和隐性基因。

  8. I have inherited my mother's dominant genes.

    我继承了我母亲的显性基因。
