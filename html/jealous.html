单词：jealous     [ˈdʒeləs]

翻译：adj. 妒忌的；小心守护的     释义：showing extreme cupidity; painfully desirous of another's advantages;
近义词：
  • anxious
  • careful
  • resentful
  • watchful
反义词：
  • indifferent
  • trustful
形近词：
    例句:
    1. He was hounded out of his job by jealous rivals.

      他受到忌妒他的竞争者的算计而被迫离职。

    2. He was said to be jealous of her.

      据说是他忌妒她。

    3. I was jealous of Mary's new bicycle.

      我很羡慕玛丽的新自行车。

    4. He is jealous of his colleague's success.

      他嫉妒同事的成功。

      你们会去崇拜一个有嫉妒心的人?
