单词：scope     [skəup]

翻译：n. （活动）范围；机会，余地     释义：an area in which something acts or operates or has power or control:
近义词：
  • application
  • confines
  • effect
  • range
反义词：
    形近词：
    • cope
    • slope
    • score
    例句:
    1. He gets a work that gives scope for his abilities.

      他得到了一份有机会发挥他才能的工作。

    2. He has no scope for his particular talents.

      他没有施展自己特殊才能的机会。

    3. It is within my scope.

      那是在我的范围之内。

    4. This subject is outside the scope of our inquiry.

      这个问题不在我们探讨的范围之内。

    5. We have to broaden our scope.

      咱们应当扩大一下眼界。

    6. The scope for human error could also be minimised.

      人为出错的机会也尽可能减低。

    7. He scopes the big picture of Israeli-Arab relations.

      他认真地查看关于以色列和阿拉伯关系的大图片。
