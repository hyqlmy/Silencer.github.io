单词：sometimes     [ˈsʌmtaimz]

翻译：adv. 不时，有时，间或     释义：on certain occasions or in certain cases but not always;
近义词：
  • at times
  • now and then
  • occasionally
反义词：
  • always
  • invariably
形近词：
  • sometime
例句:
  1. Old people are sometimes forgetful.

    老人有时爱忘事。

  2. Sometimes the patient cried for the pain.

    有时这个病人疼得哭起来。

  3. Love is sometimes put in range with a career.

    人们往往认为爱情和事业是并驾齐驱的。

  4. Sometimes she saw the flash of a torch through a chink in the curtains.

    她间或透过窗帘的缝隙能瞥见手电筒的闪光。
