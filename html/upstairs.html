单词：upstairs     [ˌʌpˈstɛəz]

翻译：adv. 楼上的     释义：the part of a building above the ground floor;
近义词：
  • upwards
反义词：
  • downstairs
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Jim lived upstairs.

      吉姆住在楼上。

    2. They tiptoed upstairs so as not to wake the baby.

      他们踮着脚上楼,生怕把孩子吵醒。

    3. The boy ran upstairs quickly.

      男孩很快地跑上楼。

    4. We crept upstairs so as not to wake the baby.

      为了不吵醒婴儿，我们蹑手蹑脚地上了楼梯。

    5. The plane tried to avoid a storm by going upstairs.

      飞机往高空飞去，以避开风暴。

    6. He is all vacant upstairs.

      他头脑里空空如也。

    7. She doesn't use the upstairs at all any more.

      她不再使用楼上了。

    8. The upstairs rooms are much warmer than those on the ground floor.

      楼上的房间要比底层的暖和得多。

    9. He is skilled in upstairs politics.

      他精通上层政治。
