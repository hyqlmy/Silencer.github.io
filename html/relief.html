单词：relief     [riˈli:f]

翻译：n. （痛苦等）减轻，解除；援救，救济     释义：the feeling that comes when something burdensome is removed or reduced;
近义词：
  • easement
  • remission
反义词：
形近词：
  • belief
例句:
  1. The pills gave her some relief.

    药片减轻她一些痛苦。

  2. This medicine will give you quick relief.

    这种药将迅速解除你的病痛。

  3. A doctor's life is consecrated to the relief of suffering.

    医师的一生奉献于解除世人的疾苦。

  4. His jokes provided some comic relief in what was really a dull speech.

    他说的笑话给极沉闷的讲话增加几分轻松气氛。

  5. The young man carved a figure in low relief.

    这个年轻人刻了一座浅浮雕人像。

  6. Relief was quickly sent to the sufferers from the great fire.

    救济品很快被送到遭受火灾的灾民手中。

  7. It was a great relief to find that my family was all safe.

    看到我的家人安然无恙，我感到极大的欣慰。

  8. The relief driver has got here.

    接班的司机已经到了。
