单词：necessity     [niˈsesiti]

翻译：n. 必要性，需要；必然性；必需品     释义：the condition of being essential or indispensable
近义词：
  • demand
  • need
  • requirement
反义词：
  • unnecessary
  • chance
形近词：
  • necessitate
例句:
  1. Necessity is the mother of invention.

    需要是发明之母。

  2. Want and necessity break faiths and oaths.

    匮乏和需要可以打破信仰和誓言。

  3. Water is of vital necessity in the desert.

    在沙漠地区水是维持生命的必需品。

  4. Food is a necessity, but wine is an extravagance.

    食物是必需品,而酒则是奢侈品。

  5. The necessity for making decisions devolves on him.

    作出决策乃当务之急，此事必然地落在他身上。

  6. That men would die was a matter of necessity; which men would die, though, was a matter of circumstance.

    人总有一死，这是必然的事，不过哪些人死，这是偶然的事。
