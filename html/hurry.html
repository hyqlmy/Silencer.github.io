单词：hurry     [ˈhʌri]

翻译：n. 急（匆）忙     释义：a condition of urgency making it necessary to hurry;
近义词：
  • hasten
  • race
  • rush
  • speed
  • accelerate
  • urge
反义词：
  • dawdle
  • linger
  • tarry
形近词：
    例句:
    1. She hurried to the airport.

      她匆匆赶往机场。

    2. He picked up his bag and hurried off along the platform.

      他拿起提包沿着站台匆忙地走了。

    3. Don't let anyone hurry you into making a decision.

      不要在任何人的催促下匆促作出决定。

    4. We'd better hurry to the station by taxi to catch the train.

      为了赶上火车,我们最好赶紧乘出租车去车站。

    5. You must hurry up or you cannot finish it in time.

      你必须赶紧些, 否则不能及时完成。

    6. In his hurry, his bike ran against a tree.

      匆忙中,他的自行车撞树上了。

    7. He was in a hurry to leave.

      他急切地要离开。

    8. You must avoid making decisions in a hurry .

      你千万不可仓促做出决定。

    9. They squeezed through the crowd in a hurry.

      他们急忙从人群中挤了过去。

    10. He put on his clothes in a hurry.

      他急忙穿上衣服。

    11. My wife went home in a hurry only to find the door locked.

      我妻子急忙回到家,结果却发现门锁着。
