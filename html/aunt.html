单词：aunt     [ɑ:nt]

翻译：n. 姨母，姑母，伯母，婶母，舅母，阿姨     释义：the sister of your father or mother; the wife of your uncle
近义词：
  • relative
  • auntie
反义词：
  • uncle
形近词：
  • amount
  • ant
例句:
  1. She has no relations besides an aged aunt.

    她除了有一个年老的伯母以外，再没有亲戚了。

  2. My aunt gave me one of her brooches as a keepsake.

    我姑母把她的一支胸针送给我作记念。

  3. I'm duty bound to visit my old aunt.

    我有义务去探望年老的姑妈。

  4. He was being terribly friendly to Aunt Liu.

    这天他对刘大婶婶态度特别友好。

  5. I'm trying to track down my old Aunt Maud.

    我正试图寻找到我那年迈的婶婶莫德。

  6. My elder sister had a baby last month, so I am an aunt now.

    我姐姐上个月生了孩子，所以我现在是阿姨了。

  7. My aunt generally come to see me on Sunday.

    我姨一般是在星期天来看我。
