单词：contradict     [ˌkɔntrəˈdikt]

翻译：v. 反驳；同…矛盾，同…抵触     释义：be in contradiction with
近义词：
  • oppose
反义词：
  • agree
  • confirm
形近词：
  • contract
  • contradiction
  • contact
例句:
  1. He did not contradict his attorney's comment.

    他没有反驳律师的意见。

  2. She doesn't like to contradict her husband in public.

    她不愿在众人面前反驳她丈夫。

  3. Don't contradict your father.

    别和你父亲犟嘴。

  4. The report contradicts what we heard yesterday.

    这个报道与我们昨天听到的有矛盾。

  5. The two statements contradict each other.

    两种说法相互抵触。
