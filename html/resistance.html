单词：resistance     [riˈzistəns]

翻译：n. （to）抵抗，反抗；抵抗力，阻力；电阻     释义：the action of opposing something that you disapprove or disagree with;
近义词：
  • opposition
  • rebellion
反义词：
  • obedience
  • support
形近词：
  • resistant
  • assistance
例句:
  1. The strategy was designed to wear down the enemy's resistance.

    这一策略旨在逐步削弱敌人的抵抗力。

  2. Her resistance to the proposal has crumpled.

    她对这个建议的抵触情绪已化为乌有了。

  3. There has been a lot of resistance to this new law.

    反对这项新法律的人很多。

  4. We anticipated that we would meet a certain amount of resistance to our plan.

    我们预料到我们的计划会遇到一些人的反对。

  5. The aircraft is streamlined to cut down wind resistance.

    飞机设计成流线型以减少风的阻力。

  6. Copper has less resistance than lead.

    铜的电阻比铅的小。
