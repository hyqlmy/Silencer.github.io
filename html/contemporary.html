单词：contemporary     [kənˈtempərəri]

翻译：adj. 现代的，当代的；同时代的     释义：a person of nearly the same age as another
近义词：
  • modern
反义词：
    形近词：
    • temporary
    • contrary
    例句:
    1. Marlowe was contemporary with Shakespeare.

      马洛与莎士比亚是同时代的。

    2. He had devoted his whole life to the study of contemporary art.

      他把他的一生都献给了当代艺术研究。

    3. Thackeray is a contemporary of Dickens.

      萨克莱跟狄更斯是同时期的人。

    4. She and I were contemporaries at college.

      她和我在学院里是同学。

    5. He is a contemporary of mine, but our experiences are completely different.

      他和我是同龄人，但我们的经历截然不同。
