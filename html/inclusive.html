单词：inclusive     [inˈklu:siv]

翻译：adj. 包括的，包含的；包容性强的，各种人都有的     释义：including much or everything; and especially including stated limits;
近义词：
  • all-in
反义词：
  • exclusive
  • narrow
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The monthly rent is $20 inclusive of everything.

      月房租总共二十美元,包括一切费用在内。

    2. His concept of history is modern and inclusive.

      她的历史观现代又富包容性。

    3. The party must adopt more inclusive strategies and a broader vision.

      这个党必须采取更广泛的策略和更远大的视野。

    4. I'll be away from the 20th to the 31st of May inclusive.

      我从五月二十号到三十一号（包括这两天）,都不会在。

    5. Does it produce a value from zero to one, inclusive or exclusive of the value '1'?

      它会产生0和1之间的值，但是否包括值'1'呢?
