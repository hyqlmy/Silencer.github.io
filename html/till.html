单词：till     [til]

翻译：prep. 直到，直到…为止     释义：unstratified soil deposited by a glacier; consists of sand and clay and gravel and boulders mixed together
近义词：
  • until
  • before
  • by
  • until
反义词：
    形近词：
    • still
    • thrill
    • ill
    例句:
    1. The meeting adjourned till five o'clock.

      会议延至五点召开。

    2. Never put off till tomorrow what you can do today.

      今日事今日毕。

    3. Not till then did I realize the danger of the situation.

      直到那个时候我才感到形势的危险。

    4. Gee, I can hardly wait till it's dark.

      哎呀，我简直等不到天黑了。

    5. We must stick to our task till it is finished.

      我们必须继续工作，直至做好为止。

    6. People do not know the value of health till they lose it.

      直到失去健康，人们才知道健康的可贵。
