单词：advisable     [ədˈvaizəbl]

翻译：adj. 可取的，适当的，明智的     释义：worthy of being recommended or suggested; prudent or wise;
近义词：
  • appropriate
  • beneficial
  • suitable
  • wise
反义词：
  • inadvisable
形近词：
  • advise
  • disable
例句:
  1. It is advisable to be forearmed.

    预作准备是明智的。

  2. This line of conduct seems advisable to me.

    这一行动方针对我来说似乎是很可取的。

  3. It is not advisable just to work away without making a timely summing-up of experience.

    一味埋头干下去不做及时的经验总结是不可取的。
