单词：invisible     [inˈvizəbl]

翻译：adj. 看不见的；隐形的     释义：impossible or nearly impossible to see; imperceptible by the eye;
近义词：
  • concealed
  • hidden
  • inappreciable
反义词：
  • visible
形近词：
  • visible
例句:
  1. Some stars are invisible to the naked eye.

    有些星体肉眼是看不见的。

  2. Germs are invisible to the naked eye.

    细菌用肉眼看不见。

  3. The mends were almost invisible.

    修补过的地方几乎看不出来。

  4. Aunt Harriet presided over an invisible army of servants that continuously scrubbed, cleaned, and published.

    姑母哈丽特支配着一大群看不见的仆人，他们不停地刷洗、打扫和擦拭。

  5. They venture past invisible boundaries at the risk of being shot.

    他们冒着被射杀的危险，穿过无形的边界。

  6. Her fantasy is to become invisible.

    她的幻想是变成隐形人。

  7. Sometimes feel as if I'm invisible, as if no one can see me at all.

    有时觉得自己像个隐形人。
