单词：southern     [ˈsʌðən]

翻译：adj. 南方的，南部的     释义：in or characteristic of a region of the United States south of (approximately) the Mason-Dixon line;
近义词：
  • southerly
反义词：
  • Northern
形近词：
    例句:
    1. He lives in southern China.

      他住在中国南部。

    2. Only in the southern part of Taiwan have I seen such scenery as this.

      唯有在南台湾我才见过像这样的风景。

    3. The secession of some southern states from the USA in the 1860s led to the civil war.

      由于南部某些州于十九世纪六十年代脱离美国而导致了南北战争。

    4. Rice is the chief crop in most southern provinces.

      稻子是大多数南方省份的主要作物。

      你喜欢北方的食物还是南方的食物?
