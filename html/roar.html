单词：roar     [rɔ:]

翻译：n.&v. 吼叫，怒号；轰鸣，咆哮     释义：a deep prolonged loud noise
近义词：
  • bellow
  • cry
  • shout
反义词：
    形近词：
    • rotary
    • oar
    • uproar
    例句:
    1. He could only roar in a gruff voice.

      他仅能以沙哑的声音吼叫着。

    2. In the picture, the dragons and tigers won't roar.

      图画里,龙不吟虎不啸（小小书僮可笑可笑）。

    3. We roar all like bears, and mourn sore like doves: we look for judgment, but there is none; for salvation, but it is far off from us.

      我们咆哮如熊，哀呜如鸽。指望公平，却是没有。指望救恩，却远离我们。

    4. He roared with pain.

      他疼得大叫起来。

    5. The roar of the train reverberated in the tunnel.

      火车的轰隆声在隧道里回荡。

    6. The murmur swelled into a roar.

      窃窃私语的声音变大形成一片喧哗。

    7. The whole room rang with a roar.

      咆哮声回荡在整个房间里。

    8. A sudden roar came from the expectant crowd.

      期待的人群中突然发出一声咆哮。

    9. The machine started up with a roar.

      机器一声轰鸣开动了。

    10. The cataract deafened the ear with its roar.

      瀑布的声音震耳欲聋。
