单词：sudden     [ˈsʌdən]

翻译：adj. 出乎意料的，突然的     释义：happening without warning or in a short space of time;
近义词：
  • abrupt
  • swift
  • unexpected
  • unforeseen
反义词：
  • slow
形近词：
    例句:
    1. His sudden death upset everybody.

      他的突然去世使大家深感悲伤。

    2. A sudden smile illuminated her face.

      突然的微笑使她容光焕发。

    3. She was dazzled by her sudden success.

      突然的成功使她感到迷惘。

    4. He gave me a fright by bursting into a sudden laughter.

      他突然大笑起来，吓了我一跳。

    5. Their sudden attack made us more aware of the danger around us.

      他们突然的攻击使我们更加注意我们周围的危险。

    6. His rise to power was very sudden.

      他的掌权很令人意外。

    7. The boy began crying all of a sudden.

      那男孩忽然哭了起来。
