单词：imagine     [iˈmædʒin]

翻译：v. 想象，设想，料想     释义：form a mental image of something that is not present or that is not the case;
近义词：
  • conceive
  • guess
  • suppose
反义词：
    形近词：
    • image
    • magazine
    例句:
    1. You can imagine how surprised I was.

      你可以想象我是多么惊讶。

    2. It is hard to imagine the scale of the universe.

      很难想像宇宙有多大。

    3. Imagine yourself to be on a desert island.

      设想一下你身处荒岛的情形。

    4. I imagine he will come tomorrow.

      我料想他明天会来。

    5. I imagine she'll have finished her homework by seven o'clock.

      我料想她会在七点之前完成作业。

    6. I can't imagine what Noah had in mind.

      我猜不出挪亚脑子里想的是什么。

    7. He imagines that people don't like him, but they do.

      他以为人们不喜欢他，但他们是喜欢他的。
