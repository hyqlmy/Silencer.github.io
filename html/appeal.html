单词：appeal     [əˈpi:l]

翻译：n. 呼吁；申诉；吸引力     释义：earnest or urgent request;
近义词：
  • request
反义词：
    形近词：
    • appal
    • appear
    例句:
    1. Their appeal for help went unheeded.

      他们求助的呼吁未受到注意。

    2. He made one last appeal to his father to forgive him.

      他最後一次恳求父亲宽恕他。

    3. His appeal to a higher court was set aside.

      他的上诉被驳回。

    4. The judge rejected the appeal made by the prisoner for a new trial.

      法官驳回了犯人提出重新审理的上诉。

    5. Jazz hasn't much appeal to me.

      爵士乐对我没有什么吸引力。

    6. Films of that sort have lost their appeal for children.

      那种电影对孩子们已经没有吸引力了。

    7. The police appealed to the crowd not to panic.

      警方向群众呼吁不要惊慌。

    8. The community is appealing to everyone to save water.

      社区呼吁每个人节约用水。

    9. Her novels appeal mostly to women.

      她的小说对妇女具有吸引力。

    10. Her sense of humour appealed to him enormously.

      她的幽默感把他强烈地吸引住了。

    11. The sinking ship appealed to the rescue team for help.

      沉船向抢救队求助。

    12. We will appeal to a great variety of source of information.

      我们将求助于各种资料信息来源。

    13. The captain appealed against the light.

      队长对光线提出异议。
