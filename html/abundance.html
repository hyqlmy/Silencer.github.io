单词：abundance     [əˈbʌndəns]

翻译：n. 丰富，充裕，大量     释义：the property of a more than adequate quantity or supply;
近义词：
  • affluence
  • plenty
反义词：
  • lack
  • scarcity
形近词：
  • abundant
例句:
  1. At the party there is food and drink in abundance.

    宴会中有丰富的食物和饮料。

  2. This country has an abundance of natural resources.

    这个国家拥有丰富的天然资源。

  3. There was an abundance of corn last year.

    去年玉米丰收。

  4. Carpets are available in abundance.

    可供地毯货源充足。

  5. The apple tree yields an abundance of fruit.

    这棵苹果树结果甚多。

  6. Medicinal herbs grow in abundance in the forest.

    这片森林里盛产药材。
