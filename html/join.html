单词：join     [dʒɔin]

翻译：v. 参加，加入；联合，连接；和…在一起     释义：the shape or manner in which things come together and a connection is made
近义词：
  • associate
  • attach
  • cement
  • combine
  • conjoin
  • connect
  • fasten
  • link
  • relate
  • unite
反义词：
  • alienate
  • dissociate
  • separate
  • sever
形近词：
  • joint
  • adjoin
例句:
  1. The island is joined to the mainland by a bridge.

    岛上有座桥与大陆相连。

  2. The two estates join at the foot of the hill.

    这两处房地产在山脚下相毗连。

  3. If you join the club, you must abide by its rules.

    你参加这个俱乐部就得遵守其规章。

  4. He was asked many times to join the party, but he always kept his distance.

    人家好几次要他参加那个政党，但他的反应总是很冷淡。

  5. He joined us in the discussion yesterday.

    他昨天参加了我们的讨论。

  6. Mary has just joined her family in Australia.

    玛丽不久前刚和她的家人在澳大利亚团聚。

  7. Mother joins me in sending you our best wishes.

    我母亲和我一起向你表示美好的祝愿。
