单词：warn     [wɔ:n]

翻译：vi. 发出警告     释义：notify of danger, potential harm, or risk;
近义词：
  • admonish
  • advise
  • alert
  • notify
反义词：
    形近词：
    • war
    例句:
    1. He warned me against going there at night.

      他警告我晚上不要到那去。

    2. They warn her that if she do it again she will be sent to prison.

      他们警告她说她再这么干就把她关进监狱。

    3. The police warn us not to go out at night.

      警察告诫我们夜间不要出门。

    4. I warned them that there might be snakes in the woods.

      我提醒他们树林里可能有蛇。

    5. If you can't arrive there on time, you should warn them.

      如果你不能按时到达，应该预先通知他们。

    6. Birds sometimes chatter to each other to warn of danger.

      鸟儿有时互相啁啾,通报危险。
