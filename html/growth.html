单词：growth     [ɡrəuθ]

翻译：n. 生长，增长，发展     释义：(biology) the process of an individual organism growing organically; a purely biological unfolding of events involved in an organism changing gradually from a simple to a more complex level;

  • advance
  • development
  • expansion

    形近词：

      市场需求低就要拖慢我们的经济增长速度。
