单词：gloomy     [ˈɡlu:mi]

翻译：adj. 阴暗的，阴沉的，令人沮丧的，阴郁的     释义：depressingly dark;
近义词：
  • depressed
  • unhappy
反义词：
  • cheerful
  • happy
  • merry
形近词：
  • loom
例句:
  1. Being alone in that gloomy house gave me the willies.

    我一个人在那所阴暗的房子心里就发毛。

  2. A few faint gleams of sunshine lit up the gloomy afternoon.

    有几束隐约的阳光使阴暗的下午有些明亮。

  3. Her smile is a breath of fresh air in this gloomy office.

    她的微笑给沉闷的办公室带来生气。

  4. There is a gloomy, overcast expression on his face.

    他愁眉苦脸。

  5. She is always in that gloomy mood.

    她总是那样情绪低沉。

  6. Share prices plunged as a result of the gloomy economic forecast.

    关于经济前景暗淡的预测导致股票价格大跌。

  7. The college graduate feels gloomy about his future.

    那位大学毕业生感到前途黯淡。
