单词：stagger     [ˈstæɡə]

翻译：n. 摇晃不稳的动作；蹒跚     释义：
近义词：
  • amaze
反义词：
    形近词：
    • stranger
    • stage
    例句:
    1. She staggered and fell.

      她踉跄了几步，跌倒在地。

    2. The drunk man staggered across the room.

      那个醉汉摇摇晃晃地穿过房间。

    3. His resolution had begun to stagger.

      他的意志开始动摇了。

    4. Such a heavy load would stagger an elephant.

      这么重的担子，大象担着也得摇晃。

    5. The unexpected blow did not stagger his resolution.

      这个意外的打击并没有动摇他的决心。

    6. I was staggered when I heard of his death.

      我听到他死的消息大吃一惊。

    7. The difficulties of the examination did not stagger him.

      考试的难度并没有使他畏缩。

    8. He gave a stagger on hearing the news.

      他听到这一消息两腿摇晃起来。

    9. They stagger from liquor, They reel in their visions, They totter in their judgments.

      他们见异象时摇摇晃晃，行审判时摇摆不定。
