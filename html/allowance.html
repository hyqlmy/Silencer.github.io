单词：allowance     [əˈlauəns]

翻译：n. 补贴，津贴；零用钱；减价，折扣；允许     释义：an amount allowed or granted (as during a given period);
近义词：
  • amount
  • contribution
  • money
反义词：
    形近词：
    • alliance
    例句:
    1. I didn't receive any allowance from my father.

      我没有收到父亲给的零用钱。

    2. I have to work to earn my allowance.

      我非得工作挣零用钱。

    3. She receives a monthly allowance of $500.

      她每月收到500美元的津贴。

    4. Your baggage allowance is 75 pounds.

      你行李的重量限额是七十五英镑。

    5. Please don't come in without my allowance.

      没有我的允许,请不要进来。

    6. The dealer gave us an allowance on our old car.

      车商给我们的旧车打了折扣

    7. She failed one of the exam papers, but we ought to make allowance for the fact that she was ill.

      她有一门不及格，但我们必须考虑到她当时有病。
