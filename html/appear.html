单词：appear     [əˈpiə]

翻译：vi. 出现；出场；问世；仿佛；出版，发表     释义：give a certain impression or have a certain outward aspect;
近义词：
  • arise
  • arrive
  • emerge
  • turn up
反义词：
  • disappear
  • evaporate
  • vanish
形近词：
  • pear
  • appeal
  • disappear
例句:
  1. A ship appeared on the horizon.

    船出现在水平线上。

  2. His book appears on the market.

    他的书出版了。

  3. An article about him appeared in the paper.

    一篇有关他的文章在报上发表。

  4. She appeared very confident.

    她显得很自信。

  5. You appear unhappy.

    你看起来好像不高兴。
