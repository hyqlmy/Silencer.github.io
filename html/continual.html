单词：continual     [kənˈtinjuəl]

翻译：adj. 不断的，连续的，频繁的     释义：occurring without interruption; chiefly restricted to what recurs regularly or frequently in a prolonged and closely spaced series;
近义词：
  • constant
  • continuous
  • endless
反义词：
  • occasional
  • temporary
形近词：
  • continue
例句:
  1. Are you tired of this continual rain?

    你对于这不停的雨不感到厌烦吗？

  2. The child's continual crying drove me to distraction.

    那孩子不断的哭声弄得我几乎要发狂。

  3. The noise of cars passing along the road is a continual disturbance to our quiet at home.

    马路上来往的车辆声音不断地破坏我们家的安宁。

  4. My work has been delayed by his continual visit.

    我的工作被他频繁的拜访给耽搁了。

  5. Our photocopier is in continual need of running repairs.

    我们的复印机经常需要检修。

  6. Continual rain ruined the crops.

    连续阴雨毁坏了庄稼。
