单词：apt     [æpt]

翻译：adj. 恰当的，适当的；易于…的，有…倾向的     释义：
近义词：
  • appropriate
  • correct
  • intelligent
反义词：
  • inapt
  • unlikely
形近词：
  • at
例句:
  1. In many ways, the comparison is apt.

    在诸多方面，这个对比是恰当的。

  2. I imagine that you may deduce an apt moral from my tale.

    我觉得你也许能从我的际遇中引出恰当的教训。

  3. Henry is an apt student of the practical science.

    亨利在实践科学上是个聪明的学生。

  4. He is a clever boy but apt to get into mischief.

    他是个聪明却很调皮的孩子。

  5. Iron is apt to rust.

    铁容易生锈。

  6. He is apt to fly out in a rage.

    他极容易发脾气。

  7. He is apt to give vent to his feelings.

    他易于吐露自己的感情。

  8. He has a handful of intelligent and apt students.

    他有许多天资聪颖的学生。
