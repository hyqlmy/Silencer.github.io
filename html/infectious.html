单词：infectious     [inˈfekʃəs]

翻译：adj. 传染的，传染性的，有感染力的     释义：caused by infection or capable of causing infection;
近义词：
  • catching
  • spreading
反义词：
  • noninfectious
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Measles is an infectious disease.

      麻疹是种传染病。

    2. When you have this anthema, you are still infectious.

      身上还有这种疹子就仍然有传染性。

    3. Flu is an infectious disease characterized by fever, aches and pains and exhaustion.

      流感是一种传染病，其特征是发热、全身疼痛和疲乏无力。

    4. He expressed infectious enthusiasm to his voters.

      他对他的选民表现出很有感染力的热情。

    5. His writing style is concise and infectious.

      他的文笔凝练，很有感染力。
