单词：destruction     [disˈtrʌkʃən]

翻译：n. 毁坏，毁灭（的原因）     释义：the termination of something by causing so much damage to it that it cannot be repaired or no longer exists
近义词：
  • extinction
  • ruin
反义词：
  • beginning
  • creation
形近词：
  • destructive
  • obstruction
  • instruction
例句:
  1. It will cause pollution and the destruction of our seas and oceans.

    这会引起污染，破坏我们的海洋和江河。

  2. Scientific knowledge was perverted to help cause destruction and war.

    科学知识被滥用于破坏和战争。

  3. Gambling was his destruction.

    赌博把他毁了。

  4. We must not bring mass destruction on ourselves by waging an atomic world war.

    我们千万不要发动世界核子战争而自招集体毁灭。

  5. The destruction of the village must have been an act of god.

    那座村庄的毁灭一定是天灾造成的。
