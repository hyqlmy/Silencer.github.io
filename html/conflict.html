单词：conflict     [ˈkɔnflikt]

翻译：v. （with）抵触，冲突     释义：an open clash between two opposing groups (or individuals);
近义词：
  • contest
  • controversy
  • disagreement
反义词：
  • agreement
  • harmony
形近词：
  • convict
例句:
  1. Both sides suffered heavy casualties in the border armed conflict.

    在这次边界武装冲突中双方都遭到了重大伤亡。

  2. He was wounded in the conflict.

    他在战斗中负伤。

  3. This is an irreconcilable conflict.

    这是一个不可调和的矛盾。

  4. He's got involved in a political conflict he can't extricate himself from.

    他陷在一场政治斗争中不能脱身。

  5. Your interests conflict with mine.

    你的利益和我的冲突。

  6. The hours of those two exams conflict.

    那两门测验的钟点互有抵触。

  7. John often comes into conflict with his boss.

    约翰经常和他的老板发生争执。

  8. A careless move may well trigger an all new debate and conflict.

    一不小心，还可能引发全新的争执和冲突。
