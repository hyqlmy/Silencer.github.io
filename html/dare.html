单词：dare     [dɛə]

翻译：v. 敢，胆敢     释义：a challenge to do something dangerous or foolhardy;
近义词：
  • challenge
  • venture
反义词：
  • shy
  • hesitate
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The gymnast dared a breathtakingly difficult move.

      这位体操选手敢于做惊险的高难度动作

    2. He dared me to jump over the river.

      他激我跳过河。

    3. He dared his grandfather's displeasure when he left the family business.

      他不顾祖父的不悦，放下了家族的生意。

    4. I would do it if I dared.

      如果我有勇气我就做。

    5. Go ahead and dive if you dare.

      如果你敢，尽管跳下水去

    6. I dare say you are right.

      我认为你是对的。

    7. I dare not touch the flower for its tenderness.

      这花太娇嫩了，我不敢碰它。

    8. If he dare say such a thing again, I shall knock him down.

      如果他胆敢再说这种话,我一定把他打倒在地。

    9. Don't worry! I am just trying my dare.

      不用担心！我只不过在试试自己的胆量而已。

    10. He only entered the competition for a dare.

      他只是因为受人激将才参加竞赛。
