单词：liable     [ˈlaiəbl]

翻译：adj. 有…倾向的；可能遭受…的；有责任的     释义：at risk of or subject to experiencing something usually unpleasant;
近义词：
  • accountable
  • answerable
  • responsible
反义词：
    形近词：
    • able
    • reliable
    例句:
    1. He declared that he was not liable for his wife's debts.

      他宣称对妻子的债务不负责。

    2. Parents are liable forchildren's education.

      父母应负责孩子的教育。

    3. They are liable to run off with your money.

      他们有卷款而走的倾向。

    4. We are liable to make mistakes.

      我们都易犯错误。

    5. A feeble attempt is liable to fail.

      愚蠢的尝试多半是要失败的。

    6. Failure to pay your taxes will make you liable to prosecution.

      不缴纳税款就可能被起诉。
