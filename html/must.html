单词：must     [mʌst]

翻译：n. 必须做的事     释义：a necessary or essential thing;
近义词：
  • have to
  • ought to
反义词：
    形近词：
      例句:
      1. Something must be done about it.

        必须得想个办法。

      2. In England traffic must keep to the left.

        在英国，车辆必须靠左行驶。

      3. We must make allowance for his youth.

        我们应当考虑到他还年轻。

      4. One must honor his contract and keep his word.

        任何人都应当尊重合同，信守诺言。

      5. The dictionary must be on the bookshelf in my bedroom.

        词典很可能丢在卧室的书架上。

      6. A raincoat is a must in the rainy season.

        在雨季雨衣是必备之物。

      7. His new novel is a must for all lovers of crime fiction.

        他的新小说是罪案小说爱好者必须一读的。
