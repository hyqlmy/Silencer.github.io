单词：irrespective     [ˌɪrisˈpektiv]

翻译：adj. 不顾的，不考虑的     释义：in spite of everything; without regard to drawbacks;
近义词：
  • regardless
  • notwithstanding
  • nevertheless
  • nonetheless
  • no matter
  • disregarding
反义词：
    形近词：
    • respective
    例句:
    1. The laws apply to everyone irrespective of race, color, or creed.

      法律对人人都适用，不分种族，肤色或信仰。

    2. We will have the work done, irrespective of cost.

      不管花多大代价,我们也要把这项工作做好。

    3. He rushed toward to help, irrespective of the consequence.

      他不顾后果，冲上前去帮助。

    4. He married her, irrespective of the will of his parents.

      他违背父母的意愿与她结了婚。
