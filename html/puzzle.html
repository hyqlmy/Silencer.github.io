单词：puzzle     [ˈpʌzl]

翻译：v. （使）迷惑，（使）为难     释义：a particularly baffling problem that is said to have a correct solution;
近义词：
  • difficulty
  • problem
  • riddle
反义词：
    形近词：
      例句:
      1. Will you help me to solve this puzzle?

        你能帮助我解决这个难题吗?

      2. He sat frowning over a crossword puzzle.

        他坐着皱着眉头在思考纵横字谜。

      3. Her decision was a puzzle to him.

        她的决定对他来说是个谜。

      4. She could not get the puzzle out of her mind.

        她无法解开心中的疑虑。

      5. He is in a puzzle about the matter.

        他对这件事大惑不解。

      6. The question puzzled me.

        这个问题把我难住了。

      7. I've never been able to puzzle her out.

        我一直对她捉摸不透。

      8. I have in mind some doubts which really puzzle me.

        我心中有一个疑团, 百思不得其解。

      9. I saw him puzzle over a math problem.

        我看见他正在苦心思索一道数学题。

      10. She listened with a puzzled expression on her face.

        她脸上带著困惑的表情在倾听。
