单词：wholly     [ˈhəuli]

翻译：adv. 完全地，全部，一概     释义：to a complete degree or to the full or entire extent (`whole' is often used informally for `wholly');
近义词：
  • all
  • completely
  • entirely
  • fully
  • totally
反义词：
  • partly
形近词：
  • holy
例句:
  1. I wholly stand for you.

    我完全支持你。

  2. I am wholly immersed in this business at present.

    我目前完全投入于这工作。

  3. You are not wholly to blame for the accident.

    你不应承担事故的全部责任。

  4. He doesn't wholly agree to the plan.

    他并不完全同意这项计划。
