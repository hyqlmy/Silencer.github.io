单词：feeble     [ˈfi:bl]

翻译：adj. 虚弱的，无力的     释义：pathetically lacking in force or effectiveness;
近义词：
  • infirm
  • weak
反义词：
  • energetic
  • healthy
  • strong
形近词：
  • feel
例句:
  1. The bishop said it with a feeble voice as he woke.

    主教醒过来，用虚弱的声音说出。

  2. Feeble and uncertain securities were thrown upon the market.

    虚弱的或者没有把握的证券都向市场抛售了。

  3. Tighter credit conditions are sapping already feeble demand.

    紧缩的信贷让本就无力的需求更加乏力。

  4. He bleated out a feeble excuse.

    他以微弱的声音作无力的辩解。
