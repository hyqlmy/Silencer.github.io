单词：inspiration     [ˌɪnspəˈreiʃən]

翻译：n. 灵感；鼓舞，激励     释义：arousal of the mind to special unusual activity or creativity
近义词：
  • encouragement
  • idea
  • motive
反义词：
  • discouragement
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Many poets and artists have drawn their inspiration from nature.

      许多诗人和艺术家从大自然中获得他们的灵感。

    2. Her speeches have ever been a fount of inspiration to her audiences.

      她的演说对于听众来说永远是灵感之源。

    3. His wife was a constant inspiration to him.

      他的妻子经常鼓励他。

    4. Let's give mutual support and inspiration to each other.

      让我们互相支持，互相鼓励。

      呼吸的过程包含了两个主要的阶段：吸气和吐气。
