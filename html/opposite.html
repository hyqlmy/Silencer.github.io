单词：opposite     [ˈɔpəzit]

翻译：n. 对立面     释义：a word that expresses a meaning opposed to the meaning of another word, in which case the two words are antonyms of each other;
近义词：
  • adverse
  • contrary
  • contrasted
  • different
  • contrary
  • converse
反义词：
  • alike
  • similar
  • same
形近词：
  • composite
  • oppose
例句:
  1. She hurried away in the opposite direction.

    她朝着相反的方向匆匆离去了。

  2. The fireplace flamed the opposite wall.

    炉火照亮了对面的墙壁。

  3. Our points of view are at opposite poles.

    我们的观点截然相反。

  4. He came in and took a seat opposite to me.

    他走进来，坐在我对面的座位上。

  5. She starred opposite Jack Nicholson in Blood and Wine.

    她在《鲜血与美酒》中与杰克·尼科尔森演对手戏。

  6. They sat opposite at the table.

    他们面对面地坐在桌子两旁

  7. There is a couple with a dog who live opposite.

    对面住着一对夫妇,他们养着一条狗。

  8. Vice and virtue are opposites.

    善和恶是对立面。

  9. You are cold-blooded,she is the opposite.

    你冷酷无情,而她恰恰相反。

  10. What I detest is that she always fancies me as her opposite.

    我讨厌她总是把我想象成她的对手。
