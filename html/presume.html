    例句:
    1. The law presumes innocence until guilt is proved.

      在罪状未证实前，法律假定被告无罪。

    2. We presume that the factory has been fully engaged.

      我们推测工厂的生产已经满负荷了。

    3. That's the new assistant, I presume.

      我想那就是新来的助理

    4. I presume that this is your final answer.

      我认为这就是你最后的答复。

    5. A signed invoice presumes receipt of the shipment.

      经过签字的发票表示货物已经收到。

    6. May I presume to advise you?

      我可以冒昧向你进一言吗？

    7. I didn't presume to interpret it.

      我不敢对这一点妄加解释。

    8. He used to presume upon his position.

      他一向滥用职权。
