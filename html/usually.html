单词：usually     [ˈjuːʒuəli]

翻译：adv. 通常，平常     释义：under normal conditions;
近义词：
  • chiefly
  • generally
  • habitually
  • traditionally
  • typically
反义词：
  • exceptionally
  • extraordinary
形近词：
  • usual
例句:
  1. I usually wake up early.

    我通常醒得很早。

  2. Medicines are usually compounds.

    药品通常是复合物。

  3. The herrings usually live in large shoals.

    青鱼通常生活在大的浅水区。

  4. They don't usually imprison first offenders.

    他们通常不监禁初次犯罪的人。

  5. A man's voice is usually lower than a woman's.

    男子的嗓音通常比女子的低。

  6. Children under five usually travel free on trains.

    五岁以下的儿童乘火车通常免费。

  7. The teacher usually worked on till late at night.

    这位老师经常一直工作到深夜。
