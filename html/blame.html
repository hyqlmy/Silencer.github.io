单词：blame     [bleim]

翻译：n. 责任，过错；责备     释义：an accusation that you are responsible for some lapse or misdeed;
近义词：
  • condemn
  • criticize
反义词：
  • credit
  • honor
形近词：
  • blaze
  • flame
  • blade
  • lame
例句:
  1. The driver was not to blame for the accident.

    这次事故怪不着司机。

  2. The bank manager was really to blame, though he tried to pin it on a clerk.

    真正受责备的应该是银行经理，可是他却想把责任推到一个职员身上。

  3. She blamed him for the failure of their marriage.

    她把婚姻的触礁归咎於他。

  4. They blamed the rise in oil prices for the big increase in inflation.

    他们把通货膨胀大幅度增长归咎于石油价格的上涨。

  5. We were ready to take the blame for what had happened.

    我们准备对所发生的事承担责任。

  6. He incurred much blame for his stubborn attitude.

    他态度顽固而招致许多责难。

  7. It was wrong of you to fasten the blame on him.

    你把过错推到他头上是不对的。

  8. She tried to shunt the blame onto Miss Williams.

    她试图把责任推至威廉姆斯小姐身上。
