单词：indifferent     [inˈdifrənt]

翻译：adj. 不感兴趣的；不关心的；冷淡的     释义：marked by a lack of interest;
近义词：
  • cool
  • insensitive
  • unconcerned
反义词：
  • concerned
  • earnest
  • earning
形近词：
  • different
例句:
  1. He is indifferent to the result of the exam.

    他对考试结果漠不关心。

  2. She seems indifferent, but deep down she's very pleased.

    她貌似无动於衷，其实心里非常高兴。

  3. Many one or little one is indifferent to me.

    多一个或少一个对我是无关紧要的。

  4. It was utterly indifferent to her who he was.

    封她来说，他是谁，根本是无关紧要的事。

  5. I'm an indifferent cook.

    我是个手艺平平的厨师。

  6. He remains indifferent in the dispute.

    他对争论表示中立。
