单词：negligible     [ˈneɡlidʒəbl]

翻译：adj. 可忽略不计的，微不足道的     释义：so small as to be meaningless; insignificant;
近义词：
  • trifling
  • trivial
  • unimportant
反义词：
  • important
  • significant
形近词：
  • intelligible
  • eligible
例句:
  1. The damage to my car is negligible.

    我的汽车的损坏是极轻微的。

  2. The rumors and slanders is negligible to me.

    流言蜚语对于我来说是可以忽略的。

  3. Details are negligible as far as I know.

    我认为这些细节完全可以忽略。

  4. The risks were represented as negligible.

    把这么冒险的事说得微不足道。

  5. He is a negligible person in the government.

    在政府中他是个微不足道的人。

  6. His knowledge of geography is negligible.

    他的地理知识少得可怜。
