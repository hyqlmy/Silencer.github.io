单词：regardless     [riˈɡɑ:dlis]

翻译：adj.&adv. 不管…的，不顾…的，不注意的     释义：(usually followed by `of') without due thought or consideration;
近义词：
  • anyhow
  • anyway
  • nevertheless
  • nonetheless
反义词：
    形近词：
      例句:
      1. He is regardless of the result.

        他不顾虑结果。

      2. He crushed the bloom with regardless tread.

        他毫不在意一脚践踏了鲜花。

      3. I protested, but she carried on regardless.

        我极力反对，但她置之不理仍一意孤行。

      4. They decorated the house regardless of cost.

        他们不惜工本装修这栋房子。

      5. All our proposals were rejected, regardless of their merits.

        我们所有的建议都遭到拒绝，不管这些建议的价值如何。
