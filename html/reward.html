单词：reward     [riˈwɔ:d]

翻译：v. （for）酬劳；酬谢     释义：a recompense for worthy acts or retribution for wrongdoing;
近义词：
  • compensation
  • payment
  • prize
反义词：
  • fine
  • punishment
形近词：
  • read
  • ward
  • regard
  • rear
例句:
  1. An act of kindness deserves a reward.

    做好事,当然要有报答。

  2. By rights, half the reward should be mine.

    按理说，有一半奖赏应该是我的。

  3. You have received a just reward.

    你已得到了应有的报酬。

  4. They rewarded the winners with gifts of fruits and flowers.

    他们奖给优胜者一些水果和鲜花。

  5. Anyone providing information which leads to the recovery of the painting will be rewarded.

    凡提供线索有助於寻回该画者可获报酬.
