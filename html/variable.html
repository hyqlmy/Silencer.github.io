单词：variable     [ˈvɛəriəbl]

翻译：n. 变量     释义：something that is likely to vary; something that is subject to variation;
近义词：
  • alterable
  • changeable
反义词：
  • constant
  • invariable
形近词：
  • available
  • amiable
例句:
  1. Prices are variable according to the rate of exchange.

    物价会随汇率而变动。

  2. His mood is as variable as the weather.

    他的情绪如天气般反覆无常。

  3. The quality of the hotel food is distinctly variable.

    这个旅馆的饭菜有时很好有时很差.

  4. The temperature is a variable in the experiment.

    在该实验中温度是个变量。

  5. With so many variables, the exact cost is difficult to estimate.

    由于有许多可变因素,很难准确地估算出成本。

  6. All these variables can affect a student's performance.

    所有这些变数都会影响学生的成绩。
