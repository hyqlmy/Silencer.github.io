单词：suspect     [səˈspekt]

翻译：n. 嫌疑犯     释义：someone who is under suspicion
近义词：
  • disbelieve
  • doubt
反义词：
  • believe
  • trust
形近词：
    例句:
    1. They suspected an ambush.

      他们怀疑有埋伏。

    2. I suspect he was lying by the boy's abnormal behaviour.

      从那个男孩的反常举动中,我怀疑他在撒谎。

    3. Just at that time, I began to suspect whether to tell her that matter.

      只是在那个时候,我开始怀疑我是否应该告诉她那件事。

    4. She has more intelligence than we suspected her to possess.

      她的智慧比我们猜想的要高。

    5. He's a prime suspect in the murder case.

      他是这次谋杀案的主要怀疑对象

    6. The police couldn't prove the suspect's guilt.

      警察不能证实嫌疑犯的罪行。

    7. His motives are highly suspect.

      他的动机是高度令人生疑的。

    8. His fitness is suspect, so we can't risk including him in the team.

      他的健康令人怀疑,所以我们不能冒险接受他入队。

    9. His statements are suspect.

      他的陈述可疑。
