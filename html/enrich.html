单词：enrich     [inˈritʃ]

翻译：v. 使富足；使肥沃；装饰；加料于；浓缩     释义：make better or improve in quality;
近义词：
  • nourish
反义词：
  • impoverish
  • deprive
形近词：
  • rich
例句:
  1. The expanding economy enriched the peasants.

    不断发展的经济使农民们富裕起来。

  2. The library was enriched with new books.

    购买新书使图书馆得到了充实。

  3. Farmers enrich the soil with organic and inorganic manures.

    农民用有机肥料和无机肥料来提高土壤的肥力。

  4. An appreciation of art will enrich your life.

    艺术欣赏将会丰富你的生活

  5. Some cream will enrich the sauce.

    在调味汁里加一些奶油会使其味道更加丰富。

  6. We should enrich our knowledge step by step.

    我们应当逐步地丰富自已的知识。

  7. It is generally thought that traveling abroad can enrich one's knowledge.

    一般认为出国旅游可增广见闻。
