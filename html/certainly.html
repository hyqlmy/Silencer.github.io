单词：certainly     [ˈsə:tnli]

翻译：adv. 一定，必定，无疑；当然，行     释义：definitely or positively (`sure' is sometimes used informally for `surely');
近义词：
  • absolutely
  • definitely
  • doubtless
  • naturally
  • of course
  • surely
  • undoubtedly
  • unquestionably
  • without question
反义词：
  • doubtfully
  • dubiously
  • questionably
形近词：
  • certainty
  • certain
例句:
  1. You are certainly a talented artist.

    你当然是位有天赋的艺术家。

  2. I'll certainly be most discreet in my conversation.

    我在谈话中当然会十分小心的。

  3. He will certainly come.

    他一定会来的。

  4. He'll certainly die if you do not send for a doctor right now.

    如果你不马上请医生,他一定会死的。
