单词：distract     [disˈtrækt]

翻译：v. 分散；使分心；打扰；使心情烦乱     释义：draw someone's attention away from something;
近义词：
  • divert
反义词：
  • attract
形近词：
  • district
  • distort
  • distant
例句:
  1. The film managed to distract me from these problems for a while.

    这部影片分散了我的注意力，让我暂时忘记了这些难题。

  2. It was all a ploy to distract attention from his real aims.

    那纯粹是障眼法，用以分散人们对他真正意图的注意力。

  3. We must change our route to distract the attention of the enemy.

    我们必须改变行军路线以转移敌人的注意力。

  4. Once she gets involved in a research, nothing can distract her mind from it.

    她一旦潜心钻研某一课题,任何事情都无法使她分心。

  5. Grief distracted him.

    悲伤使他精神错乱。
