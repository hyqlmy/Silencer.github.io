单词：bark     [bɑ:k]

翻译：n. 吠声，叫声；树皮     释义：tough protective covering of the woody stems and roots of trees and other woody plants
近义词：

反义词：
    形近词：

    例句:
    1. The dog barked when the stranger came near.

    2. An old dog does not bark for nothing.

      老狗不会无事而吠。

    3. When you speak to people, don't bark at them.

      当你和人说话时不要咆哮。

    4. Some officers bark out their orders.

      有些军官以粗暴的声音发布命令。

      他碰到墙上，肘部擦破了皮。

      那些树长着非常漂亮的白色树皮。

    7. The bark of plane trees peels off regularly.

      悬铃木的树皮到时候就脱落。

    8. We found that birch bark could serve as paper.

      我们发现桦树皮可以作纸用。
