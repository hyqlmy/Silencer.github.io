单词：correspond     [ˌkɔrisˈpɔnd]

翻译：v. 通信，（with）符合，一致；（to）相当于，对应     释义：be compatible, similar or consistent; coincide in their characteristics;
近义词：
  • agree
  • communicate
  • match
反义词：
  • differ
  • disagree
形近词：
  • corresponding
  • respond
  • correspondent
例句:
  1. Your account and hers correspond.

    你的说法跟她的说法一致。

  2. Your account of events corresponds with hers.

    你说的情况跟她说的相符。

  3. The American Congress corresponds to the British Parliament.

    美国的国会相当于英国的议会。

  4. I correspond with him regularly.

    我经常和他通信。

  5. The two sisters correspond every week.

    这两姐妹每周通信。
