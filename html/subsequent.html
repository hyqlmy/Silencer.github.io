单词：subsequent     [ˈsʌbsikwənt]

翻译：adj. 随后的，后来的     释义：following in time or order;
近义词：
  • following
  • later
反义词：
  • preceding
  • previous
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Subsequent events proved me wrong.

      後来发生的事证明我错了。

    2. He confessed to other crimes subsequent to the bank robbery.

      他供认抢劫银行案後，又坦白了其他罪行。

    3. Subsequent to its success as a play, it was made into a film.

      该剧在舞台上获得成功之後，又摄制成了电影。

    4. Subsequent events confirmed our doubts.

      后来发生的事证实了我们的怀疑。

    5. The events I'm speaking of were subsequent to the war.

      我现在说到的事情都是战后发生的。
