单词：especially     [isˈpeʃəli]

翻译：adv. 特别，尤其，格外；专门地，主要地     释义：to a distinctly greater extent or degree than is common;
近义词：
  • particularly
  • specially
反义词：
    形近词：
    • special
    • speciality
    例句:
    1. Their main exports are textiles, especially silk and cotton.

      他们的主要出口货物是纺织品，特别是丝绸和棉布。

    2. The rising sun is especially beautiful to look at from this angle.

      旭日从这个角度看起来特别美。

    3. I love the country, especially in spring.

      我喜欢这乡村，尤其是在春天。

    4. Christmas is a time of mirth, especially for children.

      圣诞节是个快乐的日子，尤其是对孩子们。

    5. Noise is unpleasant, especially when you are trying to sleep.

      噪音令人讨厌，尤其当你想睡觉的时候。
