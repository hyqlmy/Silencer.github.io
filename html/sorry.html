单词：sorry     [ˈsɔri]

翻译：adj. 对不起，抱歉的；难过，悔恨的；使人伤心     释义：feeling or expressing regret or sorrow or a sense of loss over something done or undone;
近义词：
  • apologetic
  • remorseful
  • repentant
  • pitiful
  • poor
  • shabby
反义词：
  • happy
  • wonderful
  • merry
形近词：
  • story
  • lorry
  • worry
例句:
  1. I'm frightfully sorry, but I can't see you today.

    太对不起了，我今天不能见你。

  2. I'm sorry to obtrude on you at such a time.

    我很抱歉在这个时候打扰你。

  3. Don't feel sorry about the broken glass.

    别再为那打破的杯子难过了。

  4. I'm sorry he doesn't write more regularly.

    遗憾的是他没能经常写信。

  5. Sorry, we've sold out of shirts in your size.

    对不起，你这样尺寸的衬衣都卖完了。
