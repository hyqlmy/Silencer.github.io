单词：curiosity     [ˌkjuəriˈɔsiti]

翻译：n. 好奇心；古董，古玩     释义：a state in which you want to learn more about something
近义词：
  • inquisitiveness
  • interest
  • prying
反义词：
  • indifference
  • unconcern
形近词：
    例句:
    1. The child showed a healthy curiosity.

      那孩子有好奇心，这是好现象。

    2. Her curiosity was piqued.

      她产生了好奇心。

    3. I had to explain the reasons to satisfy his curiosity.

      我只好解释原因来满足他的好奇心。

    4. The rumors excite her curiosity.

      谣言引起了她的好奇心。

    5. Just to satisfy my curiosity, how much did you pay for your car?

      我只是出于好奇，请问你买这辆车花了多少钱？

    6. Curiosity is part of the children's nature.

      好奇是儿童的天性。

    7. The old wooden skis are a curiosity.

      那副木制旧滑雪板是珍奇之物。

    8. She is so eccentric that she is regarded as a bit of a curiosity.

      她非常古怪，算是个奇人。
