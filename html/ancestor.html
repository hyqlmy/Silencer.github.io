单词：ancestor     [ˈænsistə]

翻译：n. 祖宗，祖先；原型，先驱     释义：someone from whom you are descended (but usually more remote than a grandparent)
近义词：
  • forebear
反义词：
  • descendant
形近词：
    例句:
    1. During the festival the investor investigated his ancestor.

      节日期间投资者调查了他的祖宗。

    2. They said their ancestor was a famous hunter and a crack hunter.

      他们说他们的祖宗是个有名的猎人，也是个神枪手。

    3. People who share an ancestor are called relatives.

      有同一个祖先的叫做亲戚。

    4. She has worshipped her ancestor.

      她已拜过她的祖先。

    5. One of my ancestors was a great scholar.

      我的祖先当中有一位是伟大的学者。

    6. The abacus is the ancestor of the modern computer.

      算盘是现代电脑的原型。

    7. The three species evolved from a single ancestor.

      这三种生物从同一原种进化而来。
