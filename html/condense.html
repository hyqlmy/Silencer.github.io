单词：condense     [kənˈdens]

翻译：v. （使）冷凝，（使）凝结；浓缩，压缩，简缩     释义：undergo condensation; change from a gaseous to a liquid state and fall in drops;
近义词：
  • compress
  • reduce
反义词：
  • enlarge
  • expand
形近词：
  • condemn
例句:
  1. He condense his speech to half its original length.

    他把演讲的长度浓缩了一半。

  2. You can condense milk by boiling away much of the water in it.

    煮去牛奶中大部分的水分可使它浓缩。

  3. Steam condenses into water when cooling down.

    蒸汽冷却时凝结成水。

  4. Water vapors can condense to form clouds.

    水蒸汽能凝结成云。

  5. The essay is too wordy, you'd better condense it.

    这篇文章太过冗长, 要简缩一下。
