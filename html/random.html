单词：random     [ˈrændəm]

翻译：n. 随机，随意     释义：lacking any definite plan or order or purpose; governed by or depending on chance;
近义词：
  • haphazard
  • casual
  • irregular
  • unorganized
  • accidental
  • desultory
  • aimless
  • chaotic
  • promiscuous
  • adventitious
  • occasional
  • chance
  • arbitrary
  • hit and miss
  • indiscriminate
  • unsystematic
  • unplanned
  • unintentional
  • change
反义词：
  • deliberate
  • nonrandom
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Men are exposed to more acts of random physical violence.

      男人受到更多的任意的身体强烈活动。

    2. Most people know that the outcome of a coin toss is random.

      大部分人都知道掷硬币的结果是随机的。

    3. The computer will generate apparently random numbers.

      电脑似乎将随机产生号码。

    4. This random approach is not as dubious as one might expect.

      这种随意的途径并不象人们所预期的那样无把握。

    5. All the random mingling and idle talk made him hate literary parties.

      所有随意的交往和闲谈都使他憎恨文学社。

    6. Soiled dishes were piled at random.

      脏碟子胡乱地堆放着。

    7. The librarian took a book at random from the shelf.

      图书管理员从书架上随便拿了一本书。

    8. These fluctuations are random in nature.

      这种波动的性质是任意随机的。

    9. This paper proposes a method of interference suppression for a random planar array.

      本文提出一种任意平面阵列抑制干扰的方法。

    10. Complex organs made up of separate but necessary subsystems cannot be the result of random chance.

      复杂的器官是由独立的，必要的子系统组成，而且不能随机地形成。
