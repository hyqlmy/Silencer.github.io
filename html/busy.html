单词：busy     [ˈbizi]

翻译：adj. 忙碌的；热闹的，繁忙的；占线     释义：actively or fully engaged or occupied;
近义词：
  • active
  • diligent
  • employed
  • engaged
  • hardworking
  • industrious
反义词：
  • inactive
  • indolent
  • lazy
  • unemployed
形近词：
  • buy
  • bus
例句:
  1. He is busy with some important work.

    他正忙于处理一些重要的事情。

  2. He was busy getting ready for his journey.

    他正忙于准备旅行。

  3. The shop looked out into a busy street.

    这商店濒临近一条繁忙的街道。

  4. The traffic was at its height at the busy junction.

    在繁忙的交叉路口，车辆来往正处于高峰。

  5. I tried to call Mr. Cooper, but the line was busy.

    我想给库柏先生打个电话，但一直占线。

  6. To forget his trouble, he busied himself in his garden .

    为了把烦恼忘却，他让自己忙于花园的工作。

  7. As the final examinations draw near, students begin to busy themselves with reviewing.

    当期末考试即将来临时，同学们开始忙于复习。
