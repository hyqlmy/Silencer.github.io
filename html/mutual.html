单词：mutual     [ˈmju:tʃuəl]

翻译：adj. 相互的，彼此的；共同的，共有的     释义：common to or shared by two or more parties;
近义词：
  • common
  • joint
  • shared
反义词：
  • disparate
  • uncommon
  • unshared
形近词：
    例句:
    1. Other organizations have joined the confederation for mutual benefit.

      其他组织为了共同的利益也加入了这个联盟。

    2. My friend and I have mutual enthusiasm for music.

      我和我的朋友对音乐有着共同的爱好。

    3. Let's give mutual support and inspiration to each other.

      让我们互相支持，互相鼓励。

    4. Language differences are often a barrier to mutual understanding.

      语言不同,常常阻碍到彼此的了解。

    5. Negotiations between unions and management are made more difficult by mutual distrust.

      工会和资方之间的谈判由于互不信任而愈加困难。
