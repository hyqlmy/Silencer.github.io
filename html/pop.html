单词：pop     [pɔp]

翻译：v. 突然出现     释义：an informal term for a father; probably derived from baby talk
近义词：
  • appear
  • call
反义词：
    形近词：
    • pope
    例句:
    1. The champagne cork popped when he pulled it out.

      他拔香槟酒的瓶塞时它砰的一声开了。

    2. I must pop round to baker's for a loaf of bread.

      我得到面包店去买一个面包来。

    3. The rabbits popped out as soon as we opened the hutch.

      我们一打开兔箱，兔子就突然跑出来了。

    4. He seem to pop up in the most unlikely place.

      在他似乎绝不可能到的地方,他往往就在那里出现。

    5. The balloon will pop if you put a pin in it.

      如果扎进一颗大头钉, 气球就会突然爆开。

    6. If your eyes pop, they open very wide in surprise or excitement.

      如果你的眼睛睁大,它们是因为惊奇或者兴奋而张开。

    7. He popped off a couple of shots to scare away the bear.

      为了把熊吓走他开了几枪。

    8. He likes pop; he doesn't care for classic music.

      他喜欢流行歌曲，不喜欢古典音乐。

    9. The balloon burst with a loud pop.

      气球砰地一声爆炸了。

    10. "Pop, what are you talking about?" The son screams.

      “爸爸,你在说什么呀?”儿子惊叫起来。

    11. We've got many students study pop psychology in our school.

      我们学校有很多学生学习通俗心理学。

    12. I have no interest in pop music.

      我对流行音乐不感兴趣。
