单词：imagination     [iˌmædʒiˈneiʃən]

翻译：n. 想象（力）；空想，幻觉；想象出来的事物     释义：the formation of a mental image of something that is not perceived as real and is not present to the senses;
近义词：
  • fancy
  • fantasy
反义词：
    形近词：
    • imitation
    • imaginative
    例句:
    1. That's beyond the reach of my imagination.

      那是我想象不到的。

    2. Imagination raises a man above the humdrum of life.

      想象使人从单调无聊的生活中解脱出来。

    3. These plans reveal a complete failure of imagination.

      这些计划显得毫无想象力。

    4. A sketch can stimulate one's own imagination.

      图形能促进一个人的想象力。

    5. He tried to practice upon the imagination and credulity of the public.

      他企图利用群众的空想和轻信。

    6. You didn't really see a ghost, it's only imagination.

      你并没有真地看见鬼，这仅仅是幻象。
