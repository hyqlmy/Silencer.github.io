单词：professional     [prəˈfeʃənəl]

翻译：n. 自由职业者，专业人员     释义：a person engaged in one of the learned professions
近义词：
  • expert
  • well-skilled
反义词：
  • unprofessional
形近词：
  • profession
  • procession
例句:

  1. Don't wear those clothes to work; try to look more professional!别穿这些衣服去工作，要穿得象个专业人员的样子。

  2. That doctor has been accused of professional misconduct.

    那位医生被指控违反医德。

  3. She was always punctual and professional.

    她总是准时并具有专业能力。

  4. He is a professional journalist.

    他是一位专门的新闻从业人员。

  5. We joined a professional organization for teachers.

    我们参加了一个专门的教师组织。

  6. She is a real professional.

    她是个真正的专家。
