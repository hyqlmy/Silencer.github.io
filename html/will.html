单词：will     [wil]

翻译：n. 意志；决心；愿望     释义：the capability of conscious choice and decision and intention;
近义词：
  • determination
  • mind
反义词：
    形近词：
    • ill
    例句:
    1. A country must have the will to repel any invader.

      一个国家得有决心击退任何入侵者。

    2. Free will makes us able to choose our way of life.

      自由的意志使我们可以选择自己的生活方式。

    3. The rich man left his son nothing in the will.

      那位富翁在遗嘱中什么都没给他儿子留下。

    4. She was married against her will.

      她结婚是有违本意的。

    5. I will do my best.

      我将会尽力而为。

      单单为了你,我也愿意作出牺牲。

    7. You will report to me afterward.

      你稍后要向我报告。

    8. He was convinced he could win if he willed it enough.

      如果他坚定决心,他确信自己可以赢。

    9. This happened because God willed it.

      发生了这件事是上帝的旨意。

    10. He willed most of his money to charities.

      他把钱大多遗赠给慈善机构了。

    11. Call it what you will, it's still a problem.

      不管怎么说,这仍旧是个问题。

    12. You're free to travel where you will in the country.

      在这个国家里，你想上哪儿去就可以上哪儿去。
