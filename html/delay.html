单词：delay     [diˈlei]

翻译：v.&n. 耽搁，延迟     释义：time during which some action is awaited;
近义词：
  • defer
  • detain
  • hold up
  • postpone
  • put off
  • retard
反义词：
  • advance
  • forward
  • hasten
形近词：
  • decay
  • relay
例句:
  1. It was impossible to delay her at such a crisis.

    在这样一个紧要关头来耽搁她是不可能的。

  2. We decided to delay our wedding until next year.

    我们决定把婚礼推迟到明年。

  3. Our plane was delayed by fog.

    我们的飞机因大雾而误点了。

  4. The weather is responsible for the delay.

    由于天气关系才耽搁了。

  5. Delay is not permissible, even for a single day.

    不得延误，即使一日亦不可。

  6. There was a delay of two hours before the plane took off.

    飞机起飞推迟了两个小时。
