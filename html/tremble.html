单词：tremble     [ˈtrembl]

翻译：v. 发抖，颤抖；摇动；焦虑     释义：a reflex motion caused by cold or fear or excitement
近义词：
  • shake
反义词：
    形近词：
    • resemble
    例句:
    1. His remarks struck a tremble into her heart.

      他的话令她感到不寒而栗。

    2. There was a tremble in her voice.

      她声音颤抖。

    3. The dog was all of a tremble with cold.

      这条狗冻得全身发抖。

    4. He was trembling with rage.

      他气得发抖。

    5. After the severest reprimand, he did not tremble .

      即使受到最严厉的申斥，他也不发抖。

    6. I tremble to think what's going to happen.

      我一想到即将发生的事就担心。

    7. The whole house trembled when the train went by.

      火车经过时，整座房子都在震颤。
