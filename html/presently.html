单词：presently     [ˈprezəntli]

翻译：adv. 一会儿，不久；现在，目前     释义：in the near future;
近义词：
  • directly
  • immediately
  • soon
反义词：
    形近词：
    • present
    • resent
    例句:
    1. Presently I got the whole story.

      不久我便了解了整个事件。

    2. Presently we heard a bustle, and a tread of feet.

      不久我们听到一阵喧噪声，和脚步声。

    3. The doctor will be here presently.

      医生一会儿就到。

    4. Presently she excused herself and went home.

      一会儿，她就托故告辞回家了。

    5. She is presently writing a book.

      她现在正在写一本书。

    6. I'm busy but I'll come presently.

      我现在正忙，不过我马上就来。

    7. He is presently working on the novel.

      目前他正在写这部小说。

    8. I can't give you a affirmative response presently.

      目前我没法给你一个肯定的答复。
