单词：disclose     [disˈkləuz]

翻译：v. 揭示，泄露     释义：make known to the public information that was previously known only to a few people or that was meant to be kept a secret;
近义词：
  • expose
  • reveal
  • show
  • uncover
反义词：
  • conceal
  • disguise
  • hide
  • mask
形近词：
  • discourse
  • dispose
例句:
  1. I have no intention of disclosing their names.

    我无意泄露他们的姓名。

  2. The Government disclosed that another diplomat has been arrested for spying.

    政府透露

  3. The violets disclose their petals.

    紫罗兰绽开花瓣。

  4. Science can disclose the mysteries of nature.

    科学能解开自然界的奥秘。

  5. The authorities finally disclosed the truth to the press.

    当局终于向新闻界公开了真相。
