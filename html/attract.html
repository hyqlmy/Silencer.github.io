单词：attract     [əˈtrækt]

翻译：vt. 引起注意（或兴趣等），吸引；引起；激起     释义：direct toward itself or oneself by means of some psychological power or physical attributes;
近义词：
  • captivate
  • draw
  • fascinate
  • invite
  • win
反义词：
  • repel
  • repulse
形近词：
  • attractive
  • abstract
例句:
  1. The flower show attracted large crowds this year.

    今年的花展吸引了大批观众。

  2. She gave a quiet cough to attract my attention.

    她轻轻地咳了一声好引起我注意。

  3. His comments were bound to attract criticism.

    他的评论必然会招致批评。
